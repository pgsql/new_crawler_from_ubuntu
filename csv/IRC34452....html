Detailed Description

 

Responsibilities: This is a senior software engineer position. The successful candidate will work closely with the security compliance, fraud and AML technology group. The candidate is responsible for design, develop and support various existing and new applications under the Actimize environment in the areas of compliance, AML, Fraud detection and employee surveillance in a fast paced environment.  He/She must work well under pressure and be a self-starter who can handle multiple tasks.

 

Minimum Professional and Technical Skills:  Seven 

or more years of Java and web  technology,  C/C++, Perl and UNIX script development experience is required. Five or more years of SQL, PL/SQL relational databases (Sybase, Sybase IQ and Oracle) development experience. Development experience with multi-threaded applications and fincial background from bank and brokerage backoffice with a focus on regulatory requirements is big plus.

 

Minimum Education, Certification, Training:Bachelor's degree (or higher)  in Computer Science or other similar technical degree from four-year college or university; and minimum of  7  years related experience or equivalent combination of education and experience. Prior experience in financial – brokerage or banking.

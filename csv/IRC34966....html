Detailed Description

The Credit Analyst supports the company's counterparty credit function.  Responsibilities of the position include:

  • Work with other departments, including Treasury and Stock Loan, to compile and report total counterparty exposure
  • Monitor various sources (i.e., Bloomberg, Internet, rating agencies' websites) for news stories regarding approved counterparties
  • Analyze new and existing counterparties' financial statements
  • Document analysis by completing credit write-ups

  • Ad-hoc projects
Job Requirements
  • 1-3 years of relevant experience
  • Financial analysis
  • The ability to read and interpret financial statements, including ratio analysis and relative comparisons across counterparties
  • Strong Excel skills
  • Strong communication skills, both written and oral

Job Title Administrator
Department Name LGSH
Job Requirements

The Administrator is appointed by the LGMC President/CEO to serve as the Administrator of the Facility. The Administrator carries out programs within the policies and directives of the LGSH Executive Committee and serves as a liaison between the Medical Staff and the Executive Committee. He/she insures that sound management systems are coordinated to effectively carry out the mission of the organization and meet organizational goals and objectives.

EDUCATION / TRAINING/ EXPERIENCE:

a.       Bachelor's Degree Required.

b.      Master's Degree in Healthcare, Business or Management Preferred.

c.       Candidate should have a proven track record in building a business and solid sales/marketing experience.

d.      Minimum of 5 years experience in management required.

Detailed Description
Performs administrative, clerical and other duties for the legal department with a focus on the corporate legal team.  Prepares letters, compiles information  and other materials; files documents with appropriate entities in accordance with legal requirements; maintains legal files for the legal department.  Assists with special projects and other department business on an as-needed basis
Job Requirements

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS

·       Administrative support functions within Legal Department with focus on the corporate legal team. Responsibilities will include, but not be limited to: organizing, prioritizing and summarizing the content of incoming materials, correspondence, communications, email, information and documents. 

·       Assists in preparing routine legal documents and reports, under attorney supervision

·      Preparation and filing of qualifications to do business and ensuring compliance

·      Assist with special projects

·      Maintain schedules for individuals; assist with expense reports and travel

·     Assist and prepare for Board Meetings and other meetings and conferences on own initiative; perform follow-up as directed.

·      Ensure timely communication of requests, issues, complaints, problems and other actions items

·      Other projects and duties as assigned

 

MINIMUM/REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

 

·      Minimum 3 years of executive legal administrative experience  

·      Strong interpersonal skills for interfacing with numerous internal and external executives

·      Excellent organizational and written/verbal communication skills as well as the ability to perform multiple tasks in a fast-aced and exciting environment

·      Flexibility and ability to work as part of a team of attorneys, paralegals and other legal professionals

·      Strong knowledge of MS Office is required; knowledge of MS Excel and MS PowerPoint preferred.

·      Ability to prioritize tasks, exercise sound judgment and confidentiality in maintaining all company/associate confidential information

 

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

·         Bachelor’s degree and experience within the Corporate Group of either a law firm or within a Company legal department

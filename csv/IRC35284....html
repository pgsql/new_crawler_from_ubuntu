Detailed Description

The Credit Risk Data Warehousing analyst is a member of the Credit Risk team within Etrade Bank. The primary purpose of the data warehousing team is to develop, build, populate, validate, maintain, and report on the whole loan data warehouse utilized by the Credit Risk team for business intelligence, regulatory reporting, financial reporting, statistical modeling, ad hoc analysis, and support of senior management inquiries.

 

The candidate must be excited about implementing new technologies & passionate about data quality.

 

Job Responsibilities

The analyst of credit risk data warehousing is responsible for performing:

·     Data quality and impact assessment

  • Data profiling; ability to assess and analyze large data sets for completeness and quality
  • Design and development of analytic datasets
  • Design and development of dynamic web reporting through SAS Stored Process server and SAS ODS HTML coding
  • Design and development of SAS based applications using BASE SAS and Advanced SAS.
  • Data extract, transform, and load (ETL) from both relational databases and flat-file sources
  • Identification and integration of data from a variety of sources
  • Analysis and handling of data outliers and missing values
  • Documentation of all activities
  • Maintain and improve existing processes and reports.

 

The ideal candidate will have: strong intellectual curiosity, a proactive attitude, the ability to work well independently and in a team setting, and good written and verbal communication skills. Demonstrated expertise and experience working in a dynamic environment focused on Business Intelligence and data warehousing is preferred but not required.

Detailed Description

SUMMARY

This position is within the Technology Development group responsible for developing E*TRADE’s Bank Backend and various middle-tier applications.  The individual is responsible for developing and maintaining scheduled batch jobs, and to some extent also Web Services.  She/He is expected to produce high quality code and detailed documentations to ensure that E*TRADE’s customers, both internal and external, receive the best experiences.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Support business functions through the development, implementation and maintenance of software applications, with primary responsibility of development / maintenance of scheduled / automated Batch jobs and secondary responsibility of development / maintenance of Java Web Services

·         Produce detailed software design specifications

·         Provide application support by researching Production issues and implementing resolutions / fixes in a timely manner

·         Follow Software Development Life Cycle best practices

·         Keep abreast of existing and new technologies through continuous self-improvement
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·         3+ years extensive experiences with PERL and Shell scripts

·         3+ years of application development experiences using Java in an Enterprise environment

·         Experience with relational database and SQL

·         Experience with SOAP/XML and Service Oriented Architecture / Web Services preferred

·         Ability to work independently, take ownership of assignments and drive them to completion with minimum supervision

·         Excellent interpersonal skills and team work ability

·         Good written and verbal communication skills

·         Experiences with Spring framework, iBatis and Hibernate are very desirable

·         Experience with Profile Banking Systems is preferred

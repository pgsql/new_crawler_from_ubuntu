Job Requirements

Education - Training - Experience

  • Current Registered Nurse licensure to practice nursing in the state of Louisiana.

  • Satisfactory completion of a program of studies by the American Nursing Association which is recognized by the Louisiana State Board of Nursing.

  • National certification in Family Nursing Practitioner or other Nurse Practitioner specialty care areas such as Oncology, Adult.

  • Professional knowledge of nursing theory and practice. Extensive knowledge of physical assessment, differential diagnosis, pathophysiology, and management of acute and chronic patient/family problems.

  • Current licensure and certification required by the State of Louisiana of LAC 46:XLVII.3705 AND 3707

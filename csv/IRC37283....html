Detailed Description

E*TRADE Financial is looking for a product management professional with a strong background in the product management in the Financial Services industry.  This position will be responsible for writing business requirements and functional specifications, as well as managing the product development life cycle for multiple fraud prevention projects simultaneously. Candidate must be highly motivated, extremely well organized, and have excellent communication and time management skills.

 

Responsibilities:

* Manage multiple projects on the product development roadmap for products and services designed to identify high risk transactions and mitigate fraud losses

* Coordinate products throughout their lifecycle, from strategic definition to end-of-life planning

* Gather requirements for projects from various internal teams and translate them into business and functional requirements documents, including visual mock-ups, based on industry knowledge, market research, and customer feedback

* Provide subject matter expertise on fraud prevention for various initiatives throughout the organization

* Interact closely with fraud operations, product, technology development, design, quality assurance, compliance, and customer service teams

* Manage new and existing vendor relationships
Job Requirements

* 3+ years product management experience and familiarity with standard concepts, practices, and procedures in product management

* Excellent written and verbal communication skills

* Ability to manage multiple projects, prioritize tasks, and work against multiple deadlines and objectives

* Adaptable to dynamic work environments and priorities

* Demonstrate ownership and sense of urgency in resolving inquiries and issues

* Series 7 license preferred

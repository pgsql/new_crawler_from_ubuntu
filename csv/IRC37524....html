Detailed Description

The lead director of Digital Marketing is responsible for providing strategic direction, thought leadership and management of day-to-day efforts for digital marketing initiatives for www.etrade.com.

This candidate will work closely with leaders in product management, compliance, technology, services and other marketing functions to create and maintain a unified vision of the etrade.com website.  The candidate will then put together short and long-term plans to make meaningful and continuous improvements to meet that vision while improving the etrade.com site and experience.  The candidate will oversee a team devoted to ensuring consistency and excellence relative to content, branding and cross-sell optimization and linking strategic opportunities between corporate goals and specific line of business initiatives.

 

Primary responsibilities include optimizing the visitor experience for prospective E*TRADE Financial customers providing for improved acquisition and conversion efficiency of highly qualified investors in addition to optimizing the public site experience for existing E*TRADE Financial customers for improved customer engagement, growth in customer assets and greater share of wallet.

 

In support of this role the right candidate will act as internal evangelist for key initiatives and further position E*TRADE Financial as a digital marketing leader setting the standard for best practices in user centered design, e-commerce, SEO marketing, intelligent dynamic targeting, and the creation and leverage of actionable insights from advanced Web analytics.

 

Additional responsibilities include partnering with Brand Advertising, Online Advertising, Product and Corporate Communications teams to capitalize on emerging technologies and trends in the digital space including further development of Social Media and Mobile marketing strategies.

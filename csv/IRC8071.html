Job Title Ward Clerk
Department Name St Martin Hospital
Job Requirements

JOB DUTIES:

 

Responsible for performing general clerical duties at the Nurses Station under the direction of the Staff Nurses.  Assembles and dismantles charts needed.  Chart vital signs and weights.  Check cart for new orders.  Prepares nourishment and dietary lists.  Makes new Kardex Cards.  Prepares records for admissions and discharge.  Enter ancillary service reports on patient’s chart.

 

Responsible for taking incoming calls to the Nurses Station.  Answers intercom and routes personnel to room as needed.  Delivers mail, gifts and flowers to patients.  Directs visitors to patient  rooms.  Maintains stock of forms at Nurses’ Station.  Restocks Nurses Kitchen on each shift.  May be asked to wheel patients in wheelchair.  Writes requisitions for lab and x-ray or other ancillary service.  Makes copies on copy machine as needed.  Maintains census.

 

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS/EXPERIENCE:

 

High school graduate or in senior year in high school.  Good clerical skills helpful.  On the job training.

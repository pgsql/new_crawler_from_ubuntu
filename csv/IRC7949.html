Job Title Clinical Nurse Educator
Department Name ICU
Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

To provide education and leadership to nursing personnel related to cardiovascular nursing, which will include direct nursing care to a select population in a recognized nursing specialty area. In collaboration with select nursing management/leadership, plans, guides, and directs care given by nursing personnel in cardiac arena.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Current Registered Nurse licensure to practice nursing in the State of Louisiana. 
  • Minimum of 5 years ICU experience required.
  • Minimum of 5 years in cardiovascular leadership/educational role preferred.
  • Master’s degree in nursing or related field preferred.
  • Current ACLS, BLS, PALS preferred.

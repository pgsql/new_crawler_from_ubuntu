Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

Under the supervision of the ER Manager, the ER Tech performs a variety of duties to carryout the necessary requirements for good patient care.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • High School Graduate or GED required.
  • Previous ERT or equivalent experience preferred.
  • Must maintain current BLS Certification.
  • Verbal ability is required to use and understand terminology relating to equipment and procedures done within the Emergency Department.
  • Organizational skills are required to assist with treatments.
  • Ability to function in adverse conditions.

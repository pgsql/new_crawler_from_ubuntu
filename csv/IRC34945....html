Detailed Description

Description of Position

The Manger of Learning Architecture will be responsible for supporting all aspects of the development, implementation and successful go-live of a new learning management system. This will include the design, development, integration and cross-boundary collaboration associated with implementing an enterprise-wide LMS. This person will act as the system architect responsible for creating all system structures based on best practices.

Individual must demonstrate a proven track record of implementing learning management systems at a corporation or consulting practice with the ability to provide thought leadership in the area. Must have mastery of learning management systems and technical concepts associated with implementing and sustaining an enterprise-wide LMS.

Responsibilities:

·         Develop and drive ongoing LMS strategy.

·         Select and manage learning vendors and consultants, to include evaluating and recommending vendor providers, negotiating contracts, providing project management and leadership, and critically reviewing vendor work product.

·         Ensure that the selected LMS meets the procedural and technical requirements that will allow for adequate implementation and support of E*TRADE corporate training deployment, management and reporting initiatives.

·         Lead the LMS support team and ensure key repetitive issues are identified and addressed to enhance the overall learning experience.

·         Work closely with instructional design team to develop, deploy, and manage learning paths enterprise-wide with a strong emphasis on blended learning.

·         Demonstrate a thorough knowledge of LMS systems deployment from documenting technical specifications to configuration and post go live wrap up.

·         Work with Information Technology and HRIS to ensure that the all required systems and integration tools and technical structures are in place to successfully launch and continuously enhance LMS.

·         Conduct project meetings and is responsible for project deliverables from internal and external partners.

·         Support residential, blended, and online learning programs.

·         Other duties as assigned.
Job Requirements

Qualifications:

·         Bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, learning technology, or equivalent experience.

·         Experience leading one or more successful LMS implementations.

·         Current experience with commercial Learning Management Systems (SABA, SumTotal, Plateau, etc.)

·         High proficiency in project management processes and tools, database management and reporting.

·         Understanding of modern/Web 2.0/3.0 Tools and Mobile technologies is preferred.

·         Experience managing vendor relationships and external contract resources.

·         Effective leadership skills and the ability to demonstrate team building and collaboration across business functions.

·         Effective communication, organization, and problem solving skills.

Detailed Description

·         Perform detailed business analysis on current and proposed technology solutions related to mission critical systems and applications

 

·         Utilize customer and industry data to model the impact of key initiatives and the associated opportunities and risks to the organization

 

·         Conduct research and analysis on information technology patterns and effectiveness patterns of products, services and technology solutions

 

·         Critically review analysis results seeking opportunities to optimize the Business Unit’s output and provide actionable recommendations to Business partners

 

·         Interact with management to develop project plans to achieve key business objectives.  These project plans must have clear action items and success measures

 

·         Partner with Business users, technology development, and project managers from across the enterprise to ascertain business requirements and transform those requirements into a functional specification document

 

·         Leverage interpersonal skills to work in a team environment and communicate effectively across a variety of stakeholder groups

 

·         Develop high quality presentations, documentation, and analytical materials for consumption by senior management and field teams

 
Job Requirements

·         Candidate should possess a Bachelors Degree in Business Management, Finance, CS or MIS.  Masters Degree is preferred, but equivalent experience will be considered.

 

·         General knowledge of practices for creating and delivering reports from relational databases

 

·         Advanced Excel and Visio skills

 

·         Project management experience

 

·         Demonstrate strong interest in technology, particularly a familiarity with the issues facing technology from a business perspective

 

·         Perceive change as an opportunity and have tenacious approach to deadlines and deliverables

 

·         Possess excellent analytical and problem solving skills with strong attention to detail

 

·         Work independently and efficiently, in a cross-functional matrix environment, to successfully manage multiple projects in a timely manner

 

·         Display the confidence and drive to influence and energize key decision makers at all levels within the organization through excellent verbal and written communication skills, including facilitation and presentation.

Detailed Description

This position is responsible for the successful implementation and management of several concurrent projects. Projects include the implementation of Stock Plan Administration and Employee Broker Services for Fortune 1000 corporate clients in a timely and high quality fashion. The Implementation Specialist will manage the client relationship, ensuring that they and other team members understand and perform their responsibilities, maintain a high level of client satisfaction and continuously assess project risks.

This highly visible position involves interacting with a variety of individuals, internally and externally, at all levels of the organization, including the C-Suit. Candidate must be driven and a self-starter, be organized and detail-oriented, and have excellent communication skills. This position also requires the ability to understand complex software and business concepts and to quickly absorb the business details.

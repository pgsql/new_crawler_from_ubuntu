Detailed Description

Perform changes, review and control of i) the servicer and loan data at system of record to ensure loan data accuracy and integrity, ii) operations data for operational performance and iii) overall data auditability and compliance

·         Board/review routine data/information from all servicers to ensure timeliness and accuracy

·         Function as the central clearinghouse to perform controlled data changes on the systems of record for servicer & loan data through auditable approvals and updates.

·         Function as loan systems of record secondary administrator.

·         Obtain and update credit bureau and valuation data on system of record, and recalculate appropriate current values on a periodic basis (as determined by policy)

·         Review/audit credit operations data to ensure accuracy and completeness

·         Perform routine reconciliation and audit of loan data to ensure loan data integrity, auditability and compliance

·         Collaborate with peer groups and/or service providers/vendors to effectively plan, communicate, organize and execute business objectives.
Job Requirements

Bachelor’s degree in Business Management, MIS or related field or equivalent experience; Masters preferred

·         Strong knowledge of database systems principles/design, data construction and integration of systems required.

 

·         6-8 years progressive experience in mortgage and/or consumer loan data/systems management or loan processing/ management related experience; Good planning, organizational and follow up skills; Ability to multi-task and maintain composure; strong sense of urgency with the ability to execute quickly, timely and efficiently.

·         1-3 years mortgage or consumer loan servicing experience, especially in mortgages and/or home equity.

 

·         Strong SQL knowldege required; VB preferred

·         Good knowledge of financial services operations, information delivery, change management, and action planning.

·         Demonstrated collaborative partnership skills with internal and external teams

·         Good customer service orientation, business communication, collaboration, interpersonal skills and team orientation.

·         Good business acumen and fiscal aptitude.

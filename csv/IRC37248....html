Detailed Description

Position Summary:

E*TRADE Financial is looking for a product management professional with a strong background in internet marketing product management.  This position will have the primary responsibility of developing business requirements, functional specifications and managing product development life cycle for online marketing applications. Candidate must be highly motivated, extremely well organized and have excellent leadership skills.

 

Responsibilities:

  • Manage product development of online tools designed to support marketing and client acquisition initiatives.

  • Coordinate products throughout their lifecycle, from strategic definition to end-of-life planning.

  • Gather requirements from the Marketing Department and translate them into business requirements and functional specification documents based on industry knowledge, market research, and customer feedback.

  • Interact closely with marketing, product, technology development, design, quality assurance, compliance, and customer service teams.

  • Demonstrate ownership and sense of urgency in resolving inquiries and issues.

 
Job Requirements

Qualifications:

  • 5+ years product management experience and familiarity with standard concepts, practices, and procedures in product management.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills; works well in cross-functional teams

  • Self-motivated and proactive in working with business partners to drive improvements; often contributes beyond specific scope of role

  • Strong critical thinking and analytic skills; ability to understand and translate business needs and propose solutions

  • Skilled in creating comps to show visual examples to Marketing department
  • Detail and deadline oriented; ability to manage multiple projects, prioritize and work against multiple deadlines and objectives. 

  • An energetic person with a unique blend of marketing and technical savvy.

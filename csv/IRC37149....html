Detailed Description

The Corporate Actions Senior Operations Analyst is responsible for identifying, vetting and processing Domestic and Global REORG and dividend corporate action events.

Job Requirements
  • Understanding of all types of corporate action (CA) events initiated by an issuing company

  • Utilize DTCC, Bloomberg, GCA, company publications and other sources to identify CA events

  • Monitor all CA events and critical dates which dictate when an event will expire or must be processed

  • Review CA event terms and determine accuracy

  • Ensure that CA events are set up on EBOSS (Proprietary Software)

  • Ensure the accuracy and timeliness of CA processed in customer accounts

  • Coordinate the client's instructions and process the resulting transactions

  • Physical Check Processing

  • Reconciliation

  • Dividend Reinvestment Processing

  • Research and resolve customer inquiries

  • Validate all events are processed on ADP (Broadridge) back-office books and records system

  • Ensure clients' instructions are received for voluntary CA and follow up with client services with any discrepancies

  • Confirm credit of payment from DTCC on day of payment

  • Risk Management – Identify, assess, and prioritize risks associated with CA processing. Inform Team Leader or Manager and make recommendations as applicable

  • Carry out special projects as assigned by the Team Leader or Manager

  • Ensure compliance with policies, procedures and regulatory requirements

  • Perform other duties as assigned

 

 

Qualifications

  • College degree preferred 

  • Minimum of 2 years experience in the financial or in the securities industry
  • Minimum of 3 years experience with corporate actions

  • Foreign Corporate Actions experience preferred

  • Knowledge of ADP, DTCC, Bloomberg, OCC a positive

  • Understanding of financial securities markets, financial instruments, and security valuation, as well as legal and regulatory requirements

  • Familiar with operations in a financial service environment

  • Exceptional interpersonal, organizational, and management skills

  • Strong problem resolution and analytical skills

  • Working knowledge of MS Office applications and spreadsheet proficiency

  • Exceptional communication skills both verbal and written

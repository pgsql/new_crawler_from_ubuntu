Detailed Description

SUMMARY

This is an opportunity to join a growing team with a strong innovation focus.  The Fraud, AML and Compliance group is looking for a strong Project Manager to develop project plans and direct the team through committed deliverables to ensure E*TRADE protects customers from fraud, payment, and privacy threats.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Develop project plans to direct the team through committed deliverables

·         Monitor issues, risk and budget

·         Use Project Management tool (ChangePoint)

·         Control scope, define roles, and measure quality for multiple projects

·         Be interactive in each project. Understand the issues and work towards resolution. Project managers should not just report status, but should push towards to the next step and resolve conflict

·         Be responsible for team executing against tasks. Be persistent in receiving dates from team members and holding team accountable 

·         Coordinate documentation including requirements, technical documentation, sign-offs and more

·         Attend status meetings to provide updates to the team, the business owners, and the stakeholders
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·         Actimize experience (a strong plus)

·         Excellent communication skills, verbal and written

·         Must be detail oriented, organized and able to work on several projects at one time

·         Capability of working both independently and within a group

·         Experience in project life cycle deliverables and requirements

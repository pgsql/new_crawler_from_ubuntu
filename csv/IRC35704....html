Detailed Description

SUMMARY:

E*TRADE has one of the largest Data Warehouse (Approximately 10 TB) with hundreds of process running every day and millions of transactions. We have highly motivated and dedicated team of 40+ members working towards making it a word class Data Warehouse.

 

The candidate must be a self-starter, who can work under general guidelines in a fast paced work environment.  Previous ETL (Extraction, Transformation, and Loading) experience using IBM Information server 8.x tool will be plus advantage. Experience in any other version of data stage or Other ETL tool is must.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·         Perform technical analysis under the guidance of ETL lead for new projects or any enhancements

·         Participate in a team environment for the design, development and implementation of data warehouse projects.

·         Participate in preparing design document for assign projects. 

·         Work closely with development lead and other project team members to close ETL development activities as per approved design and development standards.

·         Work with ETL Architect / Sr. Developer to close technical challenges during development.

·         Prepare and validate developer’s mappings.

·         Develop ETL Jobs as per approved design and get these reviewed by technical lead.

·         Prepare unit test cases, test data, perform unit testing and share the results with project team.

·         Fix issues raised during ETL code review

·         Debug and fix issue raised during SIT/UAT/ Warranty phases

·         Work with support team to handover ETL jobs to perform UAT and production role

·         Prepare root cause corrective action document for the ETL issues.

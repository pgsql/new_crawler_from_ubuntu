Detailed Description

Provide Hyperion and Oracle maintenance and budget support functions as well as support and/or coordinate system (Hyperion) development for several initiatives that would enhance our processes in a number of ways.  Additional responsibilities include developing and socializing toolset best practices and automation across the Business Finance organization.

 

Job responsibilities will fall into three main areas:

 

  • Oracle Maintenance/Support (ongoing)
    • Support;/back-up the Accounting team in routing maintenance of the Oracle system
    • Creating of Oracle reports and maintenance of G/L mapping
  • Hyperion
    • Provide Hyperion support for all aspects of E*Trade budget process
    • Support Business Finance and Accounting needs such as access control/security, act as Liaison to MIS, general troubleshooting, creating and modifying reports and loading of data
    • Analysis of and processing of monthly allocations
  • Systems Development (ad hoc and current queue)
    • Development and maintenace of new allocation system
    • Reviewing/general automation of existing processes and developing, socializing and implementing best practices

Detailed Description

The Network Engineer is responsible for contributing network hardware support and design expertise to the centralized market data distribution environment.

·          Provide escalated network support in a high availability, 7x24x365 environment.

·          Work with vendors to configure and support recommended configurations, hardware, and protocols.

·          Collaborate with network engineering team to ensure consistency and compliance with standards.  Meet on a regular basis with network engineering and operations staff to share knowledge and foster cohesion.

·          Provide training, monitoring tools, and documentation for turnover to operational staff.

·          Research new technologies and services as they apply to the market data infrastructure.  Participate in product evaluations and provide technical recommendations.

·          Evaluate capacity planning requirements as they pertain to networks and protocols.

·          Engineer functional designs for new projects and initiatives and present assessments to upper-level management across multiple technical organizations.
Job Requirements

·         5+ years hands-on experience with enterprise-level routers, switches, and firewalls in a redundant environment.

·         Knowledge and experience with multiple network vendors. Extensive experience designing and configuring all types of Juniper, Cisco, and Voltaire hardware and IOS.

·         Experience designing WAN environments based on both public and private carrier solutions,such as Frame Relay, point-to-point, T1, DS3, OC3, OC12, OC192, MPLS, VPLS, 10GigE, Infiniband, and encrypted tunnels over public networks.

·         Strong knowledge of networking protocols such as TCP/IP.

·         Strong knowledge of routing protocols such RIP, EIGRP, OSPF, and BGP.

·         Experience with UDP/IP and multicast data technologies such as IGMP, MSDP, PIM SM and DM, RP, BIDIR, PIM

·         Knowledge of financial feeds and protocols, ie. FIX, OUCH, ITCH, along with Market Data Vendors (MDVs) and ECNs. Experience providing network connectivity to various ECNs and or MDVs.

·         Experience with monitoring tools.

·         Experience with projects involving environment scalability and capacity planning.

·         Experience with hardware and software evaluations.

·         Experience making high-level presentations is a plus.

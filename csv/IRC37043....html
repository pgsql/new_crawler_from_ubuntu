Detailed Description

 

Position Summary:
E*TRADE Client Consultants (CCs) focus on building long-term business relationships with our clients.  These activities include advising clients on the wide range of investment products and services E*TRADE FINANCIAL offers and recommending solutions that meet their individual financial needs. CCs offer our clients a personal connection to E*TRADE, designed to help them achieve their investment goals.   The E*TRADE CC will work with clients to develop an investment plan that meets their personal goals and objectives or provide guidance to help them to be a better investor on their own.  Our Client Consultants are ready to make the client experience a great one!

Our Client Consultants are self-motivated, independent thinkers who share the common desire to build long-term client relationships. Your ability to achieve success as an E*TRADE Client Consultant is supported by the E*TRADE brand, leadership, marketing support and exceptional training.

Upon hire, our Client Consultants are assigned an existing practice of valued clients. As a Client Consultant you will be responsible for the relationship and development of the practice to ensure clients are appropriately considering all the solutions E*TRADE offers.  You will deepen these relationships by providing investment guidance and advice primarily through phone interactions and face-to-face meetings.  Additionally, you will grow the practice through proactive calls to prospects and client referrals.  To learn more about our client offerings, visit our website: http://www.etrade.com

Responsibilities:

 

  • Retain and grow assets of assigned affluent households with significant holdings at E*TRADE FINANCIAL by effectively managing an assigned client base and ensuring that all clients receive an outstanding client experience.

  • Collect client financial information, analyze and evaluate a client’s financial situation, identify investment objectives, and make investment recommendations through a comprehensive and customized financial planning process.

  • Able to reach out to valued E*TRADE clients and prospects to fully explain E*TRADE’s value proposition and deepen relationships through needs based business development efforts.

  • Must be adaptable, flexible and willing to participate in a test and learn environment to determine the quality of new lead campaigns.

  • Provide advice on a variety of investment solutions, including advantages and disadvantages to different options, designed to satisfy the client’s specific financial needs.

  • Cultivate current and prospective client relationships and identify opportunities to expand these relationships by responding to client needs with creativity and innovation.

  • Leverage the great client experience that you’ve created for them to ask for referrals to family, friends and business associates.

  • Conduct effective client portfolio reviews and propose value-added solutions to client needs

  • Maintain and update reports accurately

  • Additional duties as directed by management

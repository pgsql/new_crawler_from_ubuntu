Detailed Description

The Consulting Manager creates, develops, and closes new business opportunities for E*TRADE Corporate Services Clients and Prospects and manages those engagements through completion. These engagements include business services, financial reporting, technical services, and corporate actions.  The Consulting Manager partners closely with the Client to customize a solution that meets Client’s needs while ensuring overall profitability of the project. The Consulting Manager is also responsible for developing and delivering presentations at industry and corporate events. Extensive travel may be required to meet and deliver consulting services at Client locations.  The position also partners with other internal sales and service teams to ensure they fully understand the consulting offerings and are actively listening to their clients to identify opportunities.  The successful candidate possesses a solid understanding of the business, including market dynamics and operational drivers, as well as attention to detail, analytic focus and a commitment to the success of the organization.
Job Requirements
  • 5-10 years of client-facing experience in a B2B environment, preferably in the financial services or equity compensation industries
  • BA/BS preferably in Finance, Accounting or Business Administration
  • PC software and web application experience required including MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Access, WebEx
  • Project management, including
    • Define project scope, schedule, and price with clients, client management, and other internal stakeholders
    • Effectively communicate project expectations to team members and stakeholders
    • Set and continually manage project expectations with team members and other stakeholders.
    • Identify and manage project dependencies and critical path.
    • Plan, schedule and track project timelines and milestones.
    • Proactively manage changes in project scope, identify potential crises, and devise contingency plans.
    • Define project success criteria and disseminate them to involved parties throughout project life cycle.
  • Certified Equity Professional Level 3, Series 7, and Series 63, PMP are preferred but not required.

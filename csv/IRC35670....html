Detailed Description

SUMMARY

The Innovation Technology Team is a new group whose purpose is to support E*TRADE’s Chief Innovation Officer.  With CEO visibility, this is a great opportunity to develop new technologies that will be “game changers” in how people manage their money and investments.  This is a senior level position and it is more vertical than a typical development role, in both the technology stack and business relationships.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Build innovative products.  Software design, coding, testing, and documentation.

·         Interact with senior management teams in various cross functional areas in product management and technology.

·         Interact with 3rd party vendors and consultants providing value add to the projects.

·         Act with a high degree of self-motivation and apply new technologies to solve business needs.

Detailed Description

The Director, Investor Marketing position reports to the Director of Brokerage Marketing managing a team of 4 full-time employees focused on the acquisition, engagement and retention of mass affluent long-term investing clients.

 

The primary goal of this position is to increase profitable customer relationships and assets to drive revenue growth.  This is through driving engagement with E*TRADE’s wide array of investing solutions including (but not limited to) retirement accounts, portfolio guidance from financial consultants, professionally managed accounts as well as increasing penetration of mutual fund,  ETF and fixed Income products.

 

The ideal candidate is knowledgeable of the financial services industry, energetic and highly organized manager who thrives in a collaborative, fast-paced, results-oriented environment. 

 

§  Development and implementation of multi-channel direct campaigns to increase:

o   Penetration of retirement solutions

o   Engagement with financial consultants

o   Assets at growth

§  Development of multiple customer segments with specific goals toward increasing investment product penetration/assets per customer

§  Development of offer driven direct programs to acquire high-value client and win-back former high-value clients

§  Support and collaboration with high-end relationship management and service teams to increase client retention

§  Collaborative development of quarterly brokerage marketing and communication plans and associated budgets

§  Partners with internal communications and design teams in the implementation of multi-channel campaigns

§  Tracking and accountability of supervised programs to optimize effectiveness

 

 
Job Requirements

§  Experience with segmentation strategies and tiered client programs

 

§  Strong communication and interpersonal skills (highly collaborative)

§  Strong management and team building, with the ability to leverage and maximize existing skill sets of personnel

§  Flexibility to quickly respond to, and take advantage of, changes in the marketplace.

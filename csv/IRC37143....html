Detailed Description

Reporting to the Director of Mareting Campaig Execution & Analytics -- support the Marketing organization with reporting and analytics around performance of Direct Marketing campaigns and promotions

  • Manage a team of analysts supporting ETrade’s Direct Marketing efforts, including Trading Marketing, Investing Marketing, Bank Marketing, and Corporate Services Marketing teams.
  • Leverage analytics and insight to drive marketing strategy, value propositions and initiatives to help create growth and competitive differentiation
  • Work with Marketing Managers to understand business objectives of marketing campaigns, and factors for determining success
  • Create the solution (process & technology) for measuring campaign success
  • Responsible for data mining, data compilation, data analysis, and data reporting utilizing E*Trade’s data warehouse as well as other relevant data sources
  • Presentation of key findings from campaign analytics in a concise and structured manner to enable effective decision making – distinguish between what management needs to know and what is nice to know
  • Streamline analytical process by identifying recurring analytical tasks and working with partners to automate
  • Adhere to existing analytical standards (process, methodology, terminology), and establish new standards where appropriate
  • Exploratory analytics into a variety of topics to maximize insight into the data, uncover underlying problems, extract important variables, detect anomalies.
  • Work with business and technology partners to establish comprehensive understanding of data availability (data warehouse, models, revenue/expense calculations)
  • Manage large queue of work;  provide full transparency into delivery dates of all projects;  on-time and defect-free delivery of projects

 
Job Requirements
  • 5-7 years progressive experience in business/financial analysis positions; preferably in the financial services/brokerage industry
  • BA/BS in Business, Finance or Statistical Analysis

  • Strong analytical and quantitative skills

  • Experience managing team of analysts

  • Attention to detail and ability to manage multiple projects in a timely manner

  • Strong written and verbal communication is required along with strong presentation skills and business acumen.

  • Strong relational database experience and 2+ years of SQL and SAS experience

  • Expertise in Excel

  • Knowledge of database query tools such as MS Access , Business Objects and/or Crystal Reports a plus

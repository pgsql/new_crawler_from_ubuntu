Detailed Description

The Account Executive (AE) is responsible for identifying, cold-calling, and developing leads and pursuing sales of prospective Stock Plan Services.  For the most part, prospects will not have an existing E*trade relationship but in some cases the AE will be asked to support existing client sales efforts.  The AE will need to work independently and be solely responsible for creating and implementing a business plan for a specific territory.  Will target qualified prospects in a specific region and guide them through a complex sales process involving multiple contacts/meetings.  An AE will target prospects in size from several thousand employees to those companies listed in the Fortune 500.  Prospects are developed through multiple strategies with these companies such as cold calling, follow up on leads from marketing efforts, and working in conjunction with other E*trade partners (e.g. wealth management) to jointly prospect and sell an integrated offering.

 

The full outsourcing equity compensation solutions include but are not limited to: ESPP – employee stock purchase plans, SOP – stock option plans, RSA – restricted stock award plans, RSU – restricted stock unit award plans, and SAR – stock appreciation rights.

 

The software only opportunities will consist of exclusive and non-exclusive brokerage relationships.

 

This individual will be able to build solid relationships within the senior management ranks of their prospect companies with little guidance from management on a daily basis.  He/She will be expected to meet minimums in terms of cold calls, contact rates, and face-to-face meetings on a monthly basis. The Account Executive is expected to look for opportunities to sell our products and services and build a relationship with a company that are profitable to E*trade and create a foundation on which to sell additional products in the future.  This individual will be responsible for constructing a strategic plan designed to meet specific business needs for the prospect company.   

 

In addition, the Account Executive will work to develop contacts with Industry Consultants and other centers of influence that can be leveraged to close sales.  The AE will need to work closely with Stock Plan colleagues, including implementation, account management, marketing, product development, branches, and legal in order to ensure a successful roll-out of E*trade’s products and services to the client.

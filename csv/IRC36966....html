Detailed Description

The Equity Compensation Specialist (ECS) in the Large Market is responsible for interacting with E*TRADE’s largest and most complex clients. The ECS creates a one-to-one relationship with each client they support and is the owner of all operational activities for the client on an Outsourcing or CoSourcing relationship. The ECS, in partnership with the Client Manager, is responsible for the overall satisfaction for our clients and works with all other departments within E*TRADE to solve client and participant issues.

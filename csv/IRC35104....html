Detailed Description

 

     

·         Sales Consultant  will be responsible for supporting the Sales team in a technical role to help drive net new AUM and particpants in line with the CSG goals and obejctives. The SC role will have a focus on client presentations, coordinating appropriate resources for meetings, reviewing RFP’s, articulating E*TRADE's financial reporting and accounting capabilities, account planning, strategy and supporting AE’s in a prospecting capacity.

Job Requirements
  • Qualified candidate should have 5-7 years of experience in financial reporting, accounting methods and/or equity compensation background. Candidate must have a bachelor’s degree, with a degree in finance or accounting preferred, and possess strong presentation skills at the executive level.  

  • Attend presentations as a technical resource, including capabilities demos

    • Large Market – Face to Face

    • Mid/Emerging Market – Face-Face on an ICB but primarily conference calls via WebEx

  • Primary resource/liaison for account executives, and related business partners. (i.e. Consultants, Client Managers, ECA’s etc)

    • Helps in coordinating resources for presentations and meetings

    • Including client/FAS reference collection

    • Help research issues that require input from several groups or people (e.g. operational capabilities, workarounds)

  • Work with business partners to get operational approval and to document “out-of-box” items and the recommended solution

  • Collect second level information for Prospect Profiles

  • Review plan documents from the prospect, and add any pertinent information to plan profiles

  • Support AE in presenting prospects, plan specifics, and “out of the box” solutions at Business Review Meeting

  • Monitoring of implementation status report and actual state of implementations

  • First “reviewer” of RFPs prior to distribution to AE for review

  • Help coordinate all parties and logistics should a site visit to Alpharetta be required

  • Agreements point person

    • Initial document completed by Account Executive

    • High degree of contract knowledge as trained from Legal

  • Sales Consultant will NOT:

    • Gather “first level”  information for the Prospect Profiles

Detailed Description

SUMMARY

The Senior Staff, Software Engineering position will be responsible for leading system / business analysis, technical integration & design, development, deployment of systems, and lead technical activities throughout all phases of the SDLC in support of E*TRADE FINANCIAL – Balance Sheet Management, Accounting and Credit & Risk Management business areas.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Work with external vendors and internal business teams on integrating systems and platform, and systems setup.
  • Provide technical analysis, and lead system integration for internal and vendor software applications.
  • Work with business units and Project Manager in all phases of the SDLC.
  • Develop and integrate commercial or custom applications/database in conjunction with internal IT and external vendors.
  • Define and document functional and technical specifications.
  • Provide unit and system testing functions.
Job Requirements
  • 4-8 years progressive application analysis/integration/development and support experience for commercial and/or custom application systems environments.
  • 3-5 year experience in system/database analysis and cross platform application integration.
  • 2-3 years experience in systems development using C#, ASP.NET, Web Services, Oracle (10g/11g), MS SQL Server 2005/2008, XML, ADO.  LINQ, and Windows Workflow Foundation experience desired.
  • 1-3 years fixed income portfolio, treasury, mortgage, and/or credit/risk management system experience preferred
  • Experience with UNIX / Solaris, JAVA and SVN desired
  • Direct experience using formal life cycle standards and procedures for systems development, integration and commercial systems deployment.
  • Strong knowledge database systems principles/design, data construction and integration of applications systems.
  • Excellent customer service orientation, business communication skills and team orientation

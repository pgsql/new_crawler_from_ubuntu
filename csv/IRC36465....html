Detailed Description

SUMMARY

This is an important Project Management position responsible for managing large projects that touch across the entire enterprise.  It is critical that this person is effective working with multiple and geographically dispersed groups to coordinate their activities and bring these large projects to successful completion.  The nature of the projects range from Software Implementation to Brokerage Operations.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·                     Manage day-to-day operational, project and resource allocation issues

·                     Manage the project through all phases of the project life cycle including:  scope definition, requirements, evaluation, design, development, testing, documentation, implementation, support

·                     Organize the entire project plan including:  project coordination, scheduling, resource management, status reporting, project documentation, and identification of new requirements

·                     Create and manage project timelines and budgets. Work to communicate schedules and ensure that deadlines are met

·                     Coordinate project tasks / schedules between multiple teams and vendors.

·                     Communicate across multiple group disciplines

·                     Create and update project management artifacts:  ChangePoint tool, MS Office, Visio

·                     Facilitate team discussions, understand issues and direct toward resolution.

·                     Provide updates highlighting accomplishments, risks, issues – and mitigation plans.

·                     Solicit SME’s as appropriate.

·                     Work with Technical Leads to ensure development integrity and continuity
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·                     Deep Project Management experience in Financial Services industry

·                     Strong written and verbal communication skills.

·                     Effectively communicate with personnel at all levels.

·                     Knowledge of project management principles, practices, techniques, and tools

·                     Strong interpersonal skills and ability to deal effectively in a team environment

·                     Relate well with both technical developers, and non-technical business-users

Detailed Description

SUMMARY

Utilize your advanced web development skills developing web sites and applications that utilize widget based functionality.  A developer with expert level JavaScript skills will thrive at E*TRADE because we are continually working with new and exciting technologies.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES                              

•                 Functional design and development of RIA (internet rich applications), perform unit testing, enhance application performance, and provide troubleshooting support using AJAX, XSL, XSLT, CSS, JavaScript, DHTML and XHTML for all major browsers.

•                 Manage versioning of web assets such as graphics, (X)HTML templates, JavaScript, and style sheets in the repository; knowledge of source control systems (CVS or SVN) expected

•                 Ensure that the code meets the required development standards and is optimized for faster download

 

ADDITIONALLY:                                   

•                 Continually enhance knowledge and expertise, by staying current with existing and new technologies
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS:

•                 Minimum 7 years of professional hands on web experience with a demonstrated sense of composition and page layout

•                 Excellent knowledge of (X)HTML and JavaScript

•                 AJAX, XSLT, and DHTML proficiency

•                 Knowledge of  JavaScript frameworks and remote scripting fundamentals strongly desired (jQuery in particular)

•                 Solid practical knowledge of CSS

•                 Knowledge of DOM and DOM functions

•                 Working knowledge of electronic image formats

•                 Highly motivated, detail oriented and extremely organized with proven ability to establish priorities and execute to achieve results

•                 Ability to work under tight deadlines, independently and proactively, quickly and efficiently respond to change

•                 Strong verbal and written communication skills

•                 Please be prepared to offer code examples

 

 

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

•                 BS in Computer Science, Math, Engineering or equivalent

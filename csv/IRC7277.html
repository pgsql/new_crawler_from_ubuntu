Job Title Information Systems Specialist
Department Name Information Systems
Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

Provide first level of technical support for computer hardware and software to the user community. Provide guidance and instruction on the use of applications. Assist with the implementation of new systems, upgrades, system downtimes, and troubleshooting systems with vendors and information systems personnel.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • High school graduate or equivalent. 
  • Proficient in Windows operating systems. 
  • Excellent verbal and written communications skills necessary to work with users. 
  • Excellent problem solving skills. 
  • Ability to work with diverse teams.
  • Good time management skills.

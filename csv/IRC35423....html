Detailed Description

The Director of Customer Experience acts as a customer experience expert and strategist within Etrade.com’s Product Experience group. This person will provide a deep understanding of customer needs and goals as key drivers in the development of new web site features and functionality.  Additionally, this person will ensure that the overall needs of the organization are met in delivering a superior and consistent customer experience across all customer touch points.

User research and user-centered design methods, such as field studies, heuristic evaluations, paper prototyping, card sorting, and participatory design are used to develop an understanding of the customer’s needs. Personas and customer scenarios are developed and refined from this understanding to influence new product / service development. In addition, competitive research is combined with a strong understanding of industry and web site best practices to enrich the recommendation of customer-centered product and service recommendations.

The Director of Customer Experience drives and implements a wide range of research methods to ensure that the customer’s voice is understood and clearly communicated to other team members. Customer personas and scenarios are used to help Product Managers and Visual Designers better understand customer goals. This person also works closely with Market Researchers and Product Development teams in the integration of customer requirements into web-based solutions and usability guidelines.

 

Strategy - this individual will be responsible for crafting the overall customer experience and develop a strategy for how this team will support an experience that addressses user needs and business goals. As we look to focus our website's strategy based on experiences that are relevant and valuable in context of the products our organization provides, this person will focus on ensuring that the finished product matches our overall experience strategy.  This indvidual will need to be a progressive, on-the-spot, creative problem solver with superior knowledget of customer experience and online merchandising best practices and testing methodologies.

 

Merchandising - Another important element of this role will be to establish key relationships across the organization and promote the team as an effective means of developing products which have a customer experience component.  It is critical that the Customer Experience team is viewed as a group whom are experts on interaction design and delivering a superior customer experience, while at the same time positioning the group as key partners during the product development lifecycle.

 

Leadership - In order to fully integreat the Customer Exerpience team across the organization and drive the adoption of leveraging this team, we need an individual that will provide the appropriate leadership within this team.    This individual will need to focus on the quality of the team's work (both interaction design, visal design and user testing methodologies) as well as how the business is approaching UX design.  This indvidual will also seek new ways to improve the customer experience team and ensure that there is sufficent staffing to support the needs of the organization.

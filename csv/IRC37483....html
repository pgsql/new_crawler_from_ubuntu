Detailed Description

The Lead Credit Risk analyst is a member of the Credit Risk team within Etrade Bank. The primary purpose of the Credit Risk Analysis team is to mine the whole loan data warehouse for business intelligence related to the loan portfolio, perform customer segmentation and time series analysis, respond to ad hoc inquiries, develop new reporting, automate existing reports and support senior management.

 

The candidate must be excited about discovering trends in loan data, summarizing and presenting findings, and enhancing existing analytic processes.

 

Job Responsibilities

The credit risk analyst is responsible for performing:

·         Time series analysis on the whole loan portfolios

·         Segmentation analysis and profiling

·         Customer attrition analysis

·         Logistic regression analysis to identify key drivers of customer behavior

·         Processing, analyzing, deriving business intelligence from, and reporting on third party vendor data

·         Working closely with the data warehousing team to ensure best possible data availability and accuracy

·         Working closely with the forecasting team in a supporting role

·         Working closely with the financial reporting team to produce metrics for public disclosures

·         Supporting internal and external client’s reporting needs

·         Thorough documentation of all activities

Detailed Description

SUMMARY

We are seeking an experienced Senior Staff DBA with primary skills managing a large SQL Server (2005/2008) environment. Secondary role will be managing Oracle environment.  You will have the opportunity to work on the latest technology including a move to SQL Server 2011 planned for next year.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Provide 7x24 operational, engineering, and related infrastructure and administration services for mission-critical databases in the fast-paced E*TRADE environment for brokerage, banking, financial and other business/enterprise applications.

·         Report to Database Engineering Manager within the Data Services Infrastructure Group.

·         Strong team interaction with Data Services staff/peers, other engineering and operations group members within the IT organization, as well as with application and business development teams.

·         Provide high availability (clustering and related, including replication) solutions.

·         Manage database security related concepts and designs

·         Manage storage related concepts

·         Willingness to work on-call DBA rotations in a 7x24 environment, and expected to handle a mix of database management and engineering responsibilities.
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·         4-6 years of experience, with at least the last 3-5 years as senior/lead DBA in SQL Server environment.

·         Experience in supporting Oracle databases on Unix, Linux, and Windows environments.

·         1 to 3 years hands on experience with Oracle 10g/11g, Oracle Grid Control, PL/SQL, RAC, Data Guard and other Oracle Products.

·         Other database (MySQL, DB2) skills and experience is a plus, but solid MS-SQL Server and Oracle is first and foremost important.

·         SQL (programming/scripting) skills.

·         Hands on Windows System administration skills as relates to RDBMS. Experience with VB Script, batch script is a plus. Hands on Unix and System administration skills as relates to RDBMS (SUN/Solaris and Linux/RedHat). Experience with Unix and Perl Scripting is a plus.

·         Understanding of file system and storage concepts, and relevant working experience as required for performing database administration and engineering functions. Knowledge and experience with volume management and cluster-based systems and related product/tool suites (e.g., Veritas).

·         Familiarity and knowledge of storage technologies and architectures (SAN, NAS).

 

EDUCATION, CERTIFICATION

·         BS or MS in Computer Science or related field highly desirable.

·         Microsoft Certifications and Training or equivalent experience is desirable

·         Prior experience in a real-time transaction processing (preferably financial brokerage/banking and/or eCommerce) industry is desirable.

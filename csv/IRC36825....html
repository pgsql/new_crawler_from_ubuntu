Detailed Description

E*TRADE seeks to recruit a Director to lead the Company’s Corporate Development initiatives.  This individual will report to E*TRADE’s SVP in charge of Treasury, Portfolio and Corporate Development.  This position will be direct lead in the identification and evaluation of strategic transactions, including joint ventures, partnerships, and merger & acquisition opportunities.  This person should be a results-oriented, proactive finance professional with an ability to assess and address strategic imperatives and time-critical situations as well as manage multiple projects concurrently. This individual must possess the interpersonal skills necessary to build internal partnerships with key executives and leaders across the company.

 

Responsibilities include:

  • Researching and providing key data and insights on targets or partners that support E*TRADE’s financial and strategic objectives

  • Building complex financial models in Excel to generate insights and recommendations  

  • Determining valuation, key financial and business risks and overall fit of potential acquisitions

  • Leading due diligence activities and serving as the primary point of contact for internal and external project team members

  • Assist in developing and maintaining external relationships with commercial and investment banks
Job Requirements

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in finance or accounting, MBA in Finance or CFA a plus

  • 5-10 years of experience in Corporate Finance,  Corporate/Strategic Development

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • Experience with running a significant financial model, developing and using financial modeling tools, and applying basic decision metrics for acquisitions and mergers
  • Works effectively with groups from disparate disciplines outside of span of control  
  • Comfortable working under pressure and able to multi-task and manage competing priorities
  • Must have strong working knowledge of both corporate finance and accounting

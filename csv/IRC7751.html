Job Title Office Coordinator - OB GYN
Department Name OB/GYN - Dr. Allison Taylor
Job Requirements

This position is responsible for capturing daily patient charges; daily interaction with physicians, nurses, patients in regards to patient referrals and admissions; patient follow up post discharge; financial analysis of daily and monthly data; and front office flow for the practice.

Education and Qualifications:

  • High School Graduate or Equivalent required.
  • Medical Office Assistant/Medical Assistant Certification and/or 2 years medical experience required with 3-5 years coding experience; OR Required Bachelor of Science Degree and RHIA, RHIT, Certified Professional Coder: OR coder with three to five years coding/billing experience; OR LPN with three to five years coding experience.
  • Medical terminology experience required.
  • Medical billing experience preferred.
  • Misys software experience a plus.
  • Cerner Millennium software experience a plus.

Detailed Description

SUMMARY

We are looking for a Senior Java Developer who will work on large web based applications. The position will need to work with java development team delivering middle-tier functionality.  It will also need to participate in the design, development, deployment, and maintenance of multiple web applications. The position needs to take responsibility for comprehensive unit and functional test coverage to ensure code quality.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

The Senior Java Developer will work independently and with the team as the work required to ensure all developments are fully documented and meet high level design requirements (e.g. data, customer experience, architecture, security, quality & operations, as well as regulatory & industry standards).

 

The candidate accurately needs to report all development activity, report on development progress and issues, identifying remedial action when required to address problems and keep development on track.
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·         Degree in BS or equivalent in Computer Science.

·         At least 5 years of professional experience developing commercial or enterprise-scale software in Java.

·         Extensive Java and J2EE development experience, preferably gained on complex multi-tier web-based business applications.

·         Experience of working with JavaScript, DHTML, AJAX, CSS.

·         In depth knowledge and experience of working with Apache, Tomcat, Spring, Maven, SQL.

·         Technical understanding of Service Oriented Architecture and methodologies.

·         Experience with designing and building multi-tier web applications.

·         Experience with object oriented analysis, design and programming.

 

DESIRABLE SKILL

·         Since this position involves application integration with Salesforce.com, experience developing applications with Apex and Visualforce a plus.

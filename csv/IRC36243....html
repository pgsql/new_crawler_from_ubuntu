Detailed Description

A Senior Implementation Specialist is expected to contribute to the organizations’ growth and development in a variety of ways, including: Engagement Management, Client Management, Business Development, Process Development and People Development. This position is responsible for the successful implementation and management of several concurrent projects. Projects include the implementation of Stock Plan Administration and Employee Broker Services for Fortune 1000 corporate clients.

This highly visible position involves interacting with a variety of individuals, internally and externally, at all levels including C-Suit. The Senior Implementation Specialist will continuously assess project risks and make recommendations for improvements that will benefit the organization. Candidate must be driven and a self-starter, be organized and detail-oriented, and have excellent communication skills. This position is a senior role in an unstructured environment where deadlines are tight, responsibility is great and resources are limited. Serves as mentor and lead to other team members, and assumes oversight of team in Manager’s absence

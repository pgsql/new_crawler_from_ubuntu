Detailed Description

The supervisor of Corporate Service Operations will be primarily responsible for managing and overseeing the activity of E*TRADE CLEARING LLC's outsourcing partner (Ridge Clearing & Outsourcing).  Activity will include, but not limited to, the processing of : 

·         Employee Stock Option exercises and ESPP instructions

·         Incoming and outgoing DWAC files

·         Distribution/allocation of shares

·         Daily reconciliation of multiple suspense accounts.

 
Job Requirements

The candidate must possess excellent communication skills and have a deep understanding of employee equity compensation plans, inclusive of stock option plans, employee stock purchase plans, restricted stock awards and stock appreciation rights. 

Applicants should also have a working knowledge of brokerage operations and how the various departments may influence each other.  Additionally, he/she should be service oriented as the position requires daily communication (via conference calls and e-mail) with customer service centers and the management teams of other departments and affiliates.

This supervisory position also requires:

  • Resolving intradepartmental escalations, utilizing independent decision making
  • Willingness to work additional hours as necessary in response to increased workload
  • Ability to provide technical and regulatory expertise relating to E*TRADE’s middle and back office operating systems
  • Identification of process inefficiencies and provide solutions
  • Participation in firm projects

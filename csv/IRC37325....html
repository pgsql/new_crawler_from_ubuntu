Detailed Description
  • Support client training initiatives creating instructor-led, virtual, and online course development, as well as maintenance of current materials (Student Guides, Instructor Guides, Presentation Slides, Exercises/Games/Assessments, Job Aids, Simulations, Live Meetings, etc).

  • Create effective measurement tools to help evaluate the effectiveness of curriculum on business performance.

  • Conduct course development needs assessments and create course analysis, design documents, and storyboards.

  • Ensure that course development/revision/customization projects meet or exceed requirements

  • Communicate regularly with internal stakeholders (e.g., business development and product managers).

  • Create and maintain appropriate project management documentation.

  • Research latest theories and best practices on instructional design and adult learning.

  • Excellentcustomer service attitude and skills

  • Experiencewith assessment design and training evaluation processes.

Detailed Description

The Branch Operations Manager is responsible for providing overall branch compliance, customer service, operations and sales support and expertise for the Branch team and their clients.

 

ESSENTIAL JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

 

Service/Operation/Compliance Support

 

  • Act as onsite dedicated service/operations expert on a wide range of customer issues, directly supporting the RM team and assisting their customers with account questions issues and escalations.                     

  • Troubleshoot basic and advanced technical issues for clients.

·         Research and resolve customer issues with internal cross-functional partners.  Work with RM’s to communicate solutions directly or indirectly with clients.                     

  • Outbound client contacts in defined specialty areas: Margin calls, Account Transfers, Option Exercises, Reorganization

  • Perform key compliance and oversight activities for daily Branch activities in conjunction with the Branch Manager.

 

Sales Support

 

·         Qualify and refer opportunities to Relationship Managers as a result of client interactions.

·         Assist Branch Manager with lead flow management process.

·         Assist with CRM application maintenance; provide reporting, monitoring and information evaluation and analysis.

·         Follow-up on investment transaction status with internal management.

 

Other Miscellaneous Duties

  • Responsible for providing customer service and assistance to walk-in customers while managing the front reception desk daily, as assigned.

  • Perform secondary duties and projects as defined and assigned by Branch Manager.

  • When necessary, assist with stock certificate deposit, check deposits and complete corresponding blotters.
Job Requirements

·         A minimum of 3+ years experience in a Customer Service or Operational Support environment in the Banking and/ or Financial Services Industry.  In-person Branch/Sales environment experience is a plus.

·         Proven ability to work in a fast-paced, high-profile multi-tasking environment.  Must be flexible to shift projects as priorities change. 

·         Must be detail-oriented with a strong aptitude for problem-solving and trouble shooting simple to complex issues.

  • Must have excellent communication, organization and interpersonal skills.  Excellent customer service attitude, professionalism, and work ethic.

·         Must be able to effectively organize and prioritize work on a regular basis with minimum supervision. 

·         Must be proficient in MS Office programs, specifically MS Outlook, Word, Excel and Power Point for financial reports, presentations, and other special projects, as required; knowledge of VISIO is a plus.  Strong technical aptitude and trouble shooting skills are necessary.

  • Education or Training Equivalent to: BA/BS preferably in business administration, finance or related field.

  • Interest in sales environment and sales/relationship management processes.

  • Series 7 and 63.Series 24 and 65 or 66 are a plus.

POSITION SUMMARY:

The successful candidate will be responsible for applications system support with the Operations Improvement Department.  Suppport will span the clinical, financial, and budgeting spectrum. Strong communication skills will be necessary within the approved specifications for the success of a fully integrated, functional information system.  The successful candidate's performance will need to assure that communication is consistent, timely, and that project implementations arae cost effective. Performance efforts will need to be in alignment with organizational strategic priorities, goals, and the user community's priority needs.

EDUATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Four (4) year degree in Computer Science, Math, Management Science or Business Administration. Other degrees/experience may be considered. 
  • Three or more years of programming experience preferred. Health care systems preferred.
  • Experience with similar hardware and software used by LGMC is desirable.
  • Communications skills necessary to work with users in determining requirements of their computer systems.
  • Knowledge and/or expertise with PCs, Oracle database, SQL (PL/SQL) technology.
  • Ability to establish good rapport with users and fellow workers.
  • Must possess the cognitive, analytical, planning, organizational skills required to perform all tasks, duties and responsibilities required.
  • Must be able to achieve results in a large and complex organization, and ability to work as a team member is also essential.

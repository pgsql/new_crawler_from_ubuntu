Detailed Description

1.       Manage/Execution of Audit Process

·         With minimal supervision, perform a risk evaluation of audit area to identify key controls for review.

·         With minimal supervision, draft the audit program and execute the audit steps.

·         Draft questionnaires, spreadsheets, etc. to assist in analyzing business processes.

·         Draft audit reports to summarize audit findings.

·         Prepare workpapers, schedules, and summaries documenting audit evidence, conclusions, and findings reached.

50%

 

 

2.       Leadership

·         Provide audit project supervision role for staff auditors

·         Escalate risk issues and follow-up on resolutions

·         Identifies potential department improvement projects

·         Takes responsibility for staff auditors and own work product

·         Identifies and shares relevant business and audit topics with the audit group

30%

 

 

3.       Building Relationships

·         Partner with audit clients to identify constructive and value-added solutions to address risks identified.

·         Partners with audit clients to assist in research and other information dissemination processes.

·         Identifies opportunities for and participates in approved special projects, investigations or other significant business issues.

10%

 

 

4.       Annual Audit Planning

·         Initiate discussion on proposed areas for inclusion in the annual audit plan.

·         Lead sections of the annual risk assessment process.

10%

 
Job Requirements

Minimum Professional and Technical Skills:

·         5 years experience in auditing or similar business experience. 

·         Retail Brokerage and/or Clearing experience is preferred.

·         Ability to collect, analyze, and evaluate information pertaining to audits and the preparation of written reports of work performed.

·         Ability to communicate clearly and effectively, both orally and in writing.

·         Knowledge of financial services highly preferred (series 7)

·         Effective time management skills

Travel requirements are 20-30%.

Minimum Education, Certification, Training:

Minimum four year college degree in business. 

Preferable CPA/CIA certification or candidate.

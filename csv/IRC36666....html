Detailed Description

Applicant will be responsible for the growth and success of E*TRADE’s mortgage business sourced via its network of 300+ Financial Consultants and Advisors. 

 
Job Requirements

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:                                                                      

1.      Champion E*TRADE’s mortgage product amongst its network of Financial Consultants.  Perform numerous activities that drive increased volume and customer satisfaction including presentation of training materials, development of job aids, and branch site visits.   

2.      Ensure a high quality experience for customers and for Financial Consultants, designing processes and procedures accordingly.

3.      Oversee the pipeline of all Financial Consultant referral loans to ensure on-time closings and efficient resolution of issues.

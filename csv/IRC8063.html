Description
Job Title RN - Team Leader
Department Name 1116 CV Services, Medical (2 West)
Job Requirements

SUMMARY:

The Team Leader is responsible for the quality of care provided to their patients during their shift and functions as the care coordinator.  The successful applicant will be responsible for resource management within the department and will be responsible for upholding Leadership Standard in the department.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Current licensure as a registered nurse in the State of Louisiana. 
  • Preferred Baccalaureate Degree in Nursing, Business, Health Care Administration, or equivalent experience.
  • One (1) year experience required. 
  • Experience with role of charge nurse.
  • Certification preferred.   
  • Current CPR certification;
  • Advanced certification preferred if appropriate for area of clinical practice.
Status, Shifts and Hours Worked

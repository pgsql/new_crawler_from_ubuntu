Detailed Description

POSITION SUMMARY:

 

Please note that this role requires @ 50% travel.

Responsible for providing direction for internal and external events throughout the organization.  Executes tactics for events to obtain desired results; either supporting customer, investor, press, or employee programs. Manages leadership conferences, employee roadshows, departmental offsites, grand opening events (ETF Centers) and new “pop-up” events as assigned – includes overseeing the planning, scheduling, budget forecast/reconciliation and overall organization.  Maintains the global events calendar.

 

Establishes strategies with appropriate groups within the organization to support the internal client and/or external client as required.  Maintain relationships with site communicators and site leaders to plan and execute events that help continue to improve employee morale and communication throughout the organization.

 

In the external event industry, a senior specialist will complete early stages of contract negotiations with hotels, resorts, facilities and all other vendors. In some cases, a senior specialist will manage the entire contract process.  A senior specialist will also execute small scale external events independently from start to finish.

 

ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS:                                         

1.      Event Execution and Budgets                                    

  • Works with other departments to maintain a consistent message/image (i.e. HR, Corp Comm, Marketing, Sales, etc…)

  • Develops accurate budget forecasts for all events and programs

  • Evaluates details of all events to find cost efficiencies and recommend alternatives

  • Completes analysis of total spend vs. budget for all events and communicates accordingly with Finance as well as event hosts.

  • Codes all event invoices appropriately and ensures that finance is tracking appropriately against budgets

  • Effectively executes small scale internal and external events/programs and contributes to successful execution of large scale internal and external events/programs

  • Ensures all employees involved are prepped with all necessary event information

  • Leverages vendor relationships for cost efficiencies and consistency internally and externally

  • Executes early stages of contract negotiations with hotels, resorts, external facilities and vendors and follows through on final stages of negotiation process with supervisor.  May also manage entire contract process for certain event.

 

2.      Implement Global Events Calendar                            

  • Maintains Global Events Calendar - schedules and executes events

  • Assists in coordinating staff and resources responsible for each program

  • Interfaces with external vendors or agencies

·         Coordinates with appropriate business partners for event execution

 

3.      Communication and Public Relations                         

  • Supports the E*TRADE Financial brand and culture in the communication and delivery of all programs

  • Assists with communication channels for feedback

  • Develops follow-through methods to report positives and negatives back on each event

 

4.      Strategic planning                                                                     

  • Conducts research in new programs and new market areas as well as new locations in key cities ongoing

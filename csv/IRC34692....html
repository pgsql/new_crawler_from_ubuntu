Detailed Description

Position Summary:

Effectively organize and provide administrative support of Corporate Training and Performance.  Create systems and procedures that will ensure a positive experience for the employees and the training group.  Maintain training records in the Learning Management System (LMS), and provide analysis and reporting of training data.

 

 

·        Coordination of learning center facilities, including catering, ordering of materials, training class scheduling, etc.

 

·        Manages communications from employees which will include tracking and following up on issues and questions. 

 

·        Works with department manager on developing marketing and training initiatives, utilizes feedback tracking systems to create communications, coordinates all communications to employees, etc.

 

·        Works with department manager and trainers to establish consistencies in procedures, reporting structures, tracking and reporting methodologies, etc.

 

·        Administration of reports including monthly reporting on various training requirements and departmental activities

 

·        Training user groups on real time reporting

 

·        Responsible for all data functions including system setup for data/survey requirements, work with trainers on distribution of data collection methods, collect and evaluate date responses, etc.

 

·        Maintain LMS System including by not limited to adding and deleting users from the system, updating learner status, creating learning tracks, providing feedback and improvement recommendations, etc.

 

·         Coordinate and production of monthly/yearly training calendars.
Job Requirements

·         3-5 years administrative experience

·         Bachelor’s degree preferred

·         Minimum 1-2 years’ experience with Learning Management System

·         Demonstrated experience in creating and running data summary reports

·         Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)

·        The ability to decisively manage a high volume of work in a fast-paced, flexible environment

·        Organizational skills with a strong focus on attention to detail, meeting deadlines and accuracy

·        The ability to learn quickly and effectively prioritizes and tasks from multiple sources while maintaining a high quality and an on-time output

·        Clear and effective communication and interpersonal skills, which include writing, listening, telephone and presentation skills

·        Strong customer service skills, ability to work well with external and internal groups

·         Strong verbal and written communications skills

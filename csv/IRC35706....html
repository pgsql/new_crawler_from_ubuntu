Detailed Description

SUMMARY:

E*TRADE has one of the largest Data Warehouse (Approximately 10 TB) with hundreds of process running every day and millions of transactions. We have highly motivated and dedicated team of 40+ members working towards making it a word class Data Warehouse.

 

The candidate must be a self-starter, who can work under general guidelines in a fast paced work environment.  Previous ETL (Extraction, Transformation, and Loading) experience using IBM Information server 8.x tool will be plus advantage. Experience in any other version of Data Stage or other ETL tool is must.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES:

·         Perform technical analysis and prepare recommended ETL design approach

·         Participate in a team environment for the design, development and implementation of data warehouse projects.

·         Lead and drive a small team including offshore/offshore responsible for ETL jobs development, debugging and testing of project to meet project time lines.

·         Prepare and Present ETL design to all onsite ETL architects (Internal design review) and BIS design panel. 

·         Work closely with development lead to finalize on estimated/actual project completion dates for ETL design, development.

·         Work with BIS project manager to finalize on estimated/actual project completion dates.

·         Prepare and validate end to end business logic and mappings.

·         Develop ETL Job as per design,  prepare unit test cases, prepare test data and perform unit testing

·         Perform final code review before moving to SIT environment and fix issue in warranty phase

·         Prepare detailed design, architecture, mapping sheet and other ETL documents

·         Work with support team to handover ETL to perform UAT and production role

·         Resolve all technical issues raised during SIT/UAT/ Warranty phases

·         Prepare root cause corrective action document for the ETL issues.

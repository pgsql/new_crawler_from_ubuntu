Job Requirements

EDUCATION-TRAINING-EXPERIENCE

A.        Bachelor of Science in Physical or Biological Science with certification  in Dosimetry  and a graduate of an accredited Radiation Therapy program..

                                                                                   or

B.         A graduate of an approved affiliated university or approved affiliated hospital Medical Dosimetry and Radiation Therapy program.

                                                                                   or

C.                 A Registered Radiation Therapy Technologist with a minimum of one year of full time training in dosimetry under the direction of a Medical Physicist.

D.                 Certified in CPR within 90 days of employment.

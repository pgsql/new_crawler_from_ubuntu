Detailed Description

SUMMARY

The Senior Staff, QA Engineer will be responsible for testing the software developed at E*TRADE, by working directly with our offshore QA Team, development and business teams.  This will be accomplished by following all aspects of the Software Testing Lifecycle, providing direction, expertise and hands-on assistance to onshore/offshore test team.  The prime directive of the position will be to ensure and certify E*TRADE’s software products meet the highest standards of quality. 

 

The QA Engineer will act as onsite lead on projects and will work with the outsourced offshore testing team in meeting the testing requirements. Being a hand on position, the engineer will be involved in all aspects of testing - authoring test plan, test cases, defect logging & reporting. In addition, the QA Engineer will be a liaison to keep communication flowing between QA, Development, Operations, Project Management, and Business Departments through all stages of the SDLC.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Review functional specifications and architecture design

·         Develop and define test strategy and test plans based on product requirements and functional specifications

·         Knowledge of QA methodologies around defining test cases and mapping to requirements

·         Design and execution of test cases

·         Analyze and report defect / issues in a defect tracking system, ability to express the defects and provide detailed steps to reproduce them

·         Collect test metrics and report them in a systematic fashion

·         Provide the QA Management and the business with Go, No-Go recommendations based on the test results

·         Analyze and identify potential problems, and work closely with development & systems engineering teams to identify and resolve technical issues

·         Coordinate testing activities across geographically distributed team environments (US & India)

Work closely with Operations team to ensure successful product deployment

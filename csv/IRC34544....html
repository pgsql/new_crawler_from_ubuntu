Detailed Description
Position entails the monitoring, troubleshooting, upgrading, level 2/3 problem resolution of large WAN/LAN environment including Cisco routers, Cisco switches, firewalls, VPN and VOIP in a challenging operational environment. No administration of systems is involved – this role is for networking only and requires knowledge of Cisco networking.

Netops coordinates with network and system teams to implement network changes including building configurations for connectivity through WAN, LAN and firewalls. Maintain network devices including upgrades, physical replacement and configuration standards. Position requires good verbal and written communication to management, engineers and teammates. Capability to create concise documentation of issues, compile statistics and build reports on network performance is required.

Shift – Monday through Friday in the Network operations center in Alpharetta Georgia - flexibility required. This position operates in a team environment; self motivation, a sense of urgency and a good working ethic/attitude are a must to be successful in this environment.
Job Requirements
Candidate must demonstrate initiative, self motivation and posses a strong sense of urgency to be considered.

An understanding of Layer 2 and Layer 3 troubleshooting and integration. Exposure to firewall troubleshooting is preferred but not required. Exposure to maintaining, upgrading and configure L2/L3 Cisco switches, routers and firewalls. WAN experience with point to point, Ethernet and MPLS preferred. VOIP, VPLS and encryption experience as well as experience with network management/monitoring applications and sniffer applications in an operational environment preferred.

This position requires a Bachelor Degree or equivalent and 2+ years of hands on experience working in an enterprise operational LAN/WAN environment. CCNA preferred

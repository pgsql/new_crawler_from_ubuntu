Detailed Description

Job Requirements 

 

•         Provide top level professional service to our Corporate Clients, to ensure a positive Client experience on every call.

•         Effectively facilitate and negotiate Client questions/discussions regarding processes and requirements ensuring they meet client needs while balancing business controls and objectives

•         Manage monthly invoicing requirements for assigned client base, ensuring all products are correctly accounted for and documented per contractual commitments.

•         Ensure team members are cross-trained on assigned Clients so they can provide quality service when absent from office.  Assist team members as needed to provide administrative services to their Clients.

•         Build confidence in clients and business partners by consistently applying and demonstrating strong business knowledge regarding policies/processes and critical cross-functional, downstream impacts.

•         Participate, as needed, in product testing and validation using in-depth product knowledge to identify and document bugs as well as suggest enhancements.

•         Identify business needs & define processes that positively impact the client while minimizing the impact to the broader Corporate Client Services team.

•         Drive client satisfaction by consistently meeting SLAs, exceeding client expectations and pro-actively suggesting areas for improvement and methods to streamline processes.

•         Partner with the Account Management team and other key strategic business partners to form and further develop relationships with corporate clients.

•         Actively participate and lead, as requested, ongoing process improvement initiatives within the Operations organization drawing upon project management skills, technical expertise, cross-functional knowledge and teamwork.

•         Identify Client issues; perform detailed problem analysis, review solution alternatives and follow-through to resolution.  Apply judgment regarding criticality of issues, escalation requirements and insure ongoing documentation is updated as appropriate following resolution

•         Assist in finding areas to streamline processes, which minimize rework and manual processes through process improvements, additional training, etc.

•         Routine work is completed with minimal supervision.  Still require some assistance with more complex tasks

•         Partner and assist new and existing Equity Compensation Associates as necessary; provide performance feedback upon request.

•         Conduct client training on products and services

•         Ability to manage a large book of clients, typically 40-50 at a time

 

 
Job Requirements

Work Experience

•         A minimum of 1-5 years of experience with primary responsibility for administration and processing of client data in a B2B environment

•         A minimum of 1-3 years of experience supporting multiple client inquiries and phone calls in a B2B environment

•         Bachelor’s degree in business, finance or related field or equivalent experience required

•         Knowledge of equity compensation or Certified Equity Professional preferred

•         Familiar with Equity Edge or Salesforce.com preferred

Job Title Accounting/Materials Specialist
Department Name LGSH General and Administrative
Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

Responsible for providing accounting and clerical support to the Controller by maintaining accounting document files, including daily work and accounts payable. Responsible for the procurement of needed supplies and equipment, and the analysis and measurement of cost savings opportunities and other support duties as assigned by leadership to support our mission of quality, cost effective care. The Accounting/Materials SpecialIst will interact with all areas of the hospital in a courteous and positive manner that promotes cost control and as well as customer service.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • High School Graduate or GED required.
  • Some accounting or bookkeeping courses preferred.
  • Computer skills required.
  • The following will contribute to the success of the selected candidate:
    • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
    • Strong analytical skills.
    • Strong customer service skills.
    • Ability to use computer with various software programs.
    • Attentive to detail and accuracy.
    • Ability to work independently with little supervision required

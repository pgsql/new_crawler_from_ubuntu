Job Title Nurse Practitioner
Department Name Outpatient Surgery
Job Requirements

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:  

  • Current Registered Nurse licensure to practice in the state of Louisiana required.
  • Satisfactory completion of a program of studies accredited by the American Nursing Association which is recognized by the Louisiana State Board of Nursing required.
  • National certification in Family Nursing Practitioner or other Nurse Practitioner specialty care areas such as Gerontology, Adult or Gynecology-Obstetrics (or certification within three years of employment for new graduate) required.
  • Two years of clinical nursing experience required.
  • Professional knowledge of nursing theory and practice.
  • Extensive knowledge of physical assessment, differential diagnosis, pathophysiology, pharmacology, and management of acute and chronic patient/family problems.
  • Current licensure and certification required by the State of Louisiana of LAC 46:XLVII3705 and 3707 Advanced Practitioner of Nursing: Primary Nurse Associate required.
  • CPR certification to be obtained with 30 days of hire.
  • Falls Prevention, OSHA, and Restraints training to be obtained with in 30 days of hire.

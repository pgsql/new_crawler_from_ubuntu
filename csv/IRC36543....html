Detailed Description
Job Scope:
  • Create holistic analysis by pulling data from different data sources and websites for presentations to senior management and business unit leaders.
  • Use insights from the data to identify revenue generating opportunities and/or inform multivariate or a/b/c tests that drive fundamental improvements to the site experience.
  • Support the analytic needs various business units at E*TRADE by analyzing web traffic using web/clickstream tools such as Omniture.
  • Exhibit a high level of expertise in driving the data strategy across multiple "listening posts” (websites, surveys, testing, CRM systems, market research etc).
  • Collaborate with external partners (vendors, agencies, etc) to assist with data collection and reporting.

Typical Deliverables:
  • Effective and persuasive presentations (verbal and written) to senior management and business unit leaders.
  • Website behavior and customer experience analysis.
  • Weekly, monthly reports (excel, clickstream analytics, etc)
  • Data consolidation and validation; coordinating tags, tracking parameter implementations.
  • Business requirements synthesized from multiple sources including product managers, development teams, and functional group members; documentation relating to existing processes and suggestion / presentations for improving those processes.
Job Requirements
Professional Skills:
  • Strong analytic skills – able to work with complex data and apply critical thinking to obtain insights.
  • Strong communication skills – able to interact with all levels of business partners and technical resources; develop and deliver persuasive oral and written presentations.
  • Team player – works well in cross functional teams and resolves disagreements and conflicts constructively
  • Tech savvy – able to quickly understand new technologies and apply best practices
  • Detail and deadline oriented - extremely organized and able to manage multiple deadlines & priorities at once
  • Ability to understand and translate business needs and propose solutions to requirement/resource trade-offs, including escalation when appropriate
  • Self-motivated and proactive in working with business partners to drive improvements in both strategy and execution as well as fill key gaps/needs; often contributes beyond specific scope of role
  • Constant self-improvement, both learning from past mistakes as well as contributing to improvement in processes (technical or business) in the organization

Technical Skills:
  • Very strong Excel & PowerPoint skills (Access experience is a plus)
  • Experience working with standard clickstream analysis tools (Omniture experience is a plus)
  • Web/Database programming experience (SQL, PHP, VBA, etc) is a plus
  • SEM, PPC, and SEO experience is a plus

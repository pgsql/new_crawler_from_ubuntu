Detailed Description

Fraud Services performs outbound calls to those accounts that have been determined to be compromised and/or have been affected by fraud. Also, to speak to customers who are actively working through securing their account status with their case manager and/or customers are contacting E*Trade to report suspicious activity. Fraud Services will provide prevention, protection and educational tools and resources to help keep customers secure. They will also investigate the cause or reason for consideration of fraud to prevent false claims or intentional fraud from occurring. They will monitor all risk related vetting platforms to help prevent fraud activity from taking place. Fraud Services representatives will help strengthen the verification process to ensure we are not communicating account specific and E*Trade Financial related information to unauthorized callers.

 

o        Investigate the cause or reason behind specific fraud occurrences.

o        Provide high touch customer service and fraud counseling skills to affected customers while performing steps to secure customer accounts.

o        Validate account holder identification through document research. 

o        Analyze system data to identify fraudulent and potential fraudulent transactions and activity on client accounts.

o        Perform outbound attempts to verify suspicious trading activity and customer identification.

o        Identify operational gaps/inefficiencies and escalate accordingly to Operations Managers

o        Become an expert on policies and procedures.

o        Develop strong internal relationships with the synergies within the company.

o        Handle escalated operational or client issues

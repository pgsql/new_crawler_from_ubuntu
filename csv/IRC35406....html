Detailed Description

E*TRADE FINANCIAL Corporate Services is seeking a professional experienced in product development and architecture to fill the role of Product Architect in its Product Management team. This individual will be responsible for defining and documenting requirements for a variety of equity plan-related products and services used by our corporate clients and their plan participants. The position will primarily be focused on accounting, tax and reporting projects that impact our equity plan-related products and services. Additionally, this person will work with cross-functional teams to define and manage initiatives that involve enhancements to the Corporate Services product offering, including projects and programs aimed at enhancing revenue, gaining competitive advantages, reducing costs, insuring regulatory/statutory compliance, and/or improving service. This job entails end-to-end responsibility for the product development lifecycle for individual projects, from translating business requirements delivered by the Product Planners into detailed functional requirements, to independently working with the technology and quality assurance teams on design, implementation and testing, to handoff to the Product Support team for internal and customer rollout.  This position will also support the Product Support team as a subject matter expert for product maintenance and production issue escalation.

 

This role requires a combination of strategic and tactical problem solving, coupled with strong execution capability. The successful candidate will be an experienced self-starter and have excellent project management skills, with a proven track record of achieving success in a fast-paced, results-driven organization.

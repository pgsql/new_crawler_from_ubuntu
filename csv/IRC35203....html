Detailed Description

E*TRADE Financial is looking for a seasoned Product Management professional with a background in web-based products. This position will be responsible for managing all aspects of the product development life cycle for a suite of Active Trader products. The successful candidate will possess strong leadership, communication, organizational, and problem solving skills. Additionally, we seek creative minds who have a knack for thinking outside the box.

  • Work collaboratively with cross-functional team members to develop customer-facing solutions related to Active Trader products.
  • Articulate product ideas into well-crafted business and functional requirements documents.
  • Proactively engage developers, UI designers, marketing, operations, and service teams throughout the entire product rollout cycle.
  • Stay abreast of the competitive landscape for purposes of maintaining ongoing competitive analysis and idealizing innovative new feature-sets.
  • Define, plan, and execute on a product roadmap in alignment with business strategy.
  • Demonstrate ownership and sense of urgency in resolving issues.
Job Requirements
  • Candidate must have a proven track record of developing and driving multiple product initiatives from concept to implementation.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and adapt to a dynamic work environment.
  • Excellent writing skills related to business and functional requirements.
  • Experience with waterfall AND agile deployment methodologies.
  • Experience working on web-based desktop software applications strongly preferred.
  • Experience within the online brokerage industry a plus.
  • 4+ years of product management experience required.
  • Bachelor's degree required.

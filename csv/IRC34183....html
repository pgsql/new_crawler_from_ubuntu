Detailed Description

The Senior Staff, Project Management position will be responsible for leading various IT and business teams through system / business analysis, technical integration & design, development, deployment of systems, and manage all project activities throughout all phases of the SDLC. This position will be responsible for technology related projects in E*TRADE Financial – Business Systems, Accounting, Finance, Human resources, Procurement & Legal business areas.

 

•       Work with all related IT & Business teams to drive project tasks/milestones to completion. 

•       Manage all project deliverables and be responsible for keeping the team accountable to deliver on schedule.

•       Insure proper project documentation is created for each phase of the SDLC.

•       Coordinate project status by providing timely communication to all team members, business owners and stakeholders.

•       Work with external vendors and internal business teams on cross project dependencies and schedule coordination.

•       Provide/coordinate system testing functions.
Job Requirements

•       3-4+ years of project management experience.

•       Have a solid understanding of application analysis/integration/development and support experience for commercial and/or custom application system environments.

•       Strong knowledge of database system principles/design, data construction and cross platform application integration.

•       Must be detail oriented, organized and able to work on several projects at one time.

•       Proven ability managing large scale and highly complex projects, controlling project scope, costs, and delivery schedules

•       Oracle e-Business Applications experience as a project manager for new implementations and/or application upgrades

•       Functional and Technical knowledge implementing Oracle e-Business Suite applications.

•       Direct experience using formal life cycle standards and procedures for systems development, integration and commercial systems deployment.

•       Excellent customer service orientation, business communication skills and team orientation.

•       Proficient in Microsoft Project, Office and Visio.

•       Bachelor’s degree.

•       PMP and/or Oracle technical certification Desired

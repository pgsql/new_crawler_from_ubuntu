Job Title Critical Care Clinical Nurse Specialist
Department Name ICU
Job Requirements
The successful candidate will provide direct nursing care to a select population in a recognized nursing specialty area. Will plan, guide, and direct care given by other nursing personnel.

Education/Training/Qualifications:

  • Current Registered Nurse licensure to practice nursing in the state of Louisiana.
  • Specialist licensure to practice advance practice registered nursing in the state of Louisiana.
  • Current licensure and certification required by the LA. State Board of Nursing in Clinical Nurse Specialist.
  • Clinical nurse specialist role indicates a concentration/focus in Cardiovascular/Adult Critical Care Nursing either by primary education or experience.
  • Two years previous experience as Clinical Nurse Specialist preferred.
  • Professional knowledge of nursing theory and practice.

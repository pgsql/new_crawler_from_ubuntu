Detailed Description

SUMMARY

This position is part of the Technology Development group and carries responsibility for developing E*TRADE’s various middle-tier applications and Core Banking application.  It is responsible for leading a software development team, mainly to develop and maintain middle-tier Java Web Services that provide data services and business logic to E*TRADE’s various front-end applications.

  

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Lead team to review business requirements from various Business stakeholders (Product Management, Operation, Customer Services, etc) and work with various Technology teams to design and develop technology solutions that fulfill the requirements on time with excellent quality.

·         Provide technical direction for the architecture, design and development of SOA-based web services and their integration with various enterprise applications.

·         Identify project risks and work with management and the development team to avoid and/or mitigate the risks.

·         Lead team to decompose work, identify all activities, their sequence and dependencies. Proactively manage the dependencies to ensure that they are not causing project delays.

·         Oversee the resolution of Production issues; ensure that the team thoroughly researches and identifies root cause, and implements fixes in a timely manner.

·         Follow technology trends closely and keep self abreast of existing and new technologies through continuous self-improvement.

·         Provide status reports on activities, progress, and issues to management in a timely manner.

·         Oversee the development of multiple concurrent projects and monitor progress of team members to ensure that the team delivers products on time.
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·            10+ years of application development experience using Java in an Enterprise environment that includes 4+ years of management / leadership experience.

·            5+ years of experience with SOA (Service Oriented Architecture), Web Services, SOAP/XML, Spring, Hibernate, Data Modeling / Database design, SQL, Axis2, Apache CXF, Maven, Apache Tomcat, SVN

·            Excellent understanding of software development life cycle and methodology (waterfall, Agile, etc), including maintenance and production support.

·            Ability to influence and work effectively with various stakeholders: product manager, business analysts, various technology teams, software QA, system engineer, etc.

·            Excellent interpersonal skills and ability to work well with others in a team environment

·            Able to translate business requirements into concrete deliverables and develop a plan to achieve those requirements; this includes the ability to objectively evaluate business needs and the capabilities of the development staff

·            Strong conceptual thinking and the ability to engage in open discussion to find optimal solutions

·            BS in Computer Science, Software Engineering or equivalent

Company Overview

In 1983, the first online trade was placed using early E*TRADE technology.  Today, E*TRADE FINANCIAL is a global financial leader, delivering value and innovation to millions of customers in more than 40 countries worldwide.

 

We are looking for highly energized, dynamic professionals who are ready to join a fast-paced, world leader in the financial services industry. We move at the speed of the Internet.  Our work culture fosters an incredible exposure to innovative ideas and challenges that create experience and opportunity.  Many have risen through the organization to become industry leaders, in both the financial services world and in the community at large.  E*TRADE is made up of some of the brightest and most talented people in the industry - are you ready to join the industry leader?
Detailed Description

We are looking for a seasoned product management professional with a background in investment products and services and a focus on managed accounts and annuities.  Successful candidate will possess strong communication, leadership, influencing, and problem-solving skills. Desired candidate is someone who thrives in a team-oriented, results-oriented, and customer-focused environment, welcomes challenge, embraces change, is creative, can learn fast, and works well under tight deadlines.    

This product manager will assist in defining our long term investing product roadmap, and would own end to end execution towards assigned objectives.   

 

Duties & Responsiblities

 

E*TRADE’s target long term investing customers are serious investors in the mass affluent segment and expect market leading solutions.  Candidate must be able to assess these customer’s needs, identify revenue upside and cost saving opportunities, define detailed business and functional specifications, and work within a cross-functional team (engineering, operations, customer service, sales, marketing, and business) to manage an end-to-end rollout of projects.  Candidate must be highly motivated, extremely organized, detail-oriented, have web experience, and possess outstanding communication and customer service skills.   

 

Key responsibilities for this job are:

·         Write business and functional requirements necessary to develop new programs or enhance existing programs to improve customer acquisition, engagement and retention.

·         Work closely with cross functional teams, including Design, Editorial, Compliance, Technology, Web Development, Training, and Marketing to design the solutions needed, and to manage rollout of products and services in a planned and timely manner.

·         Identify and create necessary reports, metrics, and analysis to measure the effectiveness and validate the success of projects completed.

·         Evaluate customer and sales needs, and analyze the competitive environment and industry trends to define and execute on those needs.

·         Develop and present in-depth product training materials for Sales & Customer Service.

·         Understand legislative changes and implement necessary updates to training materials, forms, applications, and website pages.

·         Manage external vendor relationships as necessary.
Job Requirements

·         A comprehensive understanding of product and project management practices.

·         Hands-on/team-player attitude toward tackling tactical as well as strategic work. Proven teamwork skills to help deliver on-time product and project results.  Experience with web-based solutions.

·         Three plus years of experience as a product manager in a financial services firm and a good working knowledge of managed accounts and retirement income products, plans, market and regulations.

·         Candidates should be able to clearly articulate key business objectives, and primary metric measures for financial products and services.

·         Strong analytics, strategic thinking and consumer empathy a must.

·         Excellent written, oral, and presentation skills. 

·         BA/BS in business, management, marketing or related field required. Industry designations, such as CFP, CIMA, and/or Life & Health licenses are a plus.

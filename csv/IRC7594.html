Job Title Pharmacy Tech
Department Name 2371 Pharmacy - BRACC
Job Requirements
EDUCATION/TRAINING/EXPERIENCE:
1. High School Diploma or equivalent required.                                  
2. Must be 18 years of age.
3. Must possess a valid Louisiana Technician Certificate/License (renewable annually in June) OR
4. If a Technician Candidate:
 a. Must possess a temporary permit
b. Must satisfactorily complete a training program approved by the Louisiana Board of Pharmacy.
c. Upon completion of training program, incumbent must pass with a minimum of 75 the State Board Pharmacy Tech Certification examination before Candidate Application expires.

Description
Job Title Nurse Manager
Department Name Cyberknife
Job Requirements

Graduate from an accredited school for registered nursing, bachelor’s degree preferred or equivalent experience.         Currently licensed by the Louisiana State Board of Nurse Examiners.   Experience in Oncology or Radiation Oncology is preferred.   Certified in CPR at the time of hire or within 30 days of employment.

Provides leadership and direction for services and staff in the Cyberknife department. Monitors and reviews services and directs staff in the delivery of high quality care. Acts as an Oncology patient advocate throughout the CyberKnife treatment process and follow-up. Coordinates all pre-treatment and post-treatment procedures/exams and physician treatment schedules. Assesses, educates, and provides support to the Oncology patient, family members and staff. Assists physician with patient exams, minor procedures and sedations.
Status, Shifts and Hours Worked
Full-time; 7:30am-4 pm

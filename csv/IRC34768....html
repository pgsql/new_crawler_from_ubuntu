Detailed Description

Overview

E*TRADE Corporate Services provides equity compensation services to over 1200 corporate clients and over 1 million plan participants.  The Product Architect is responsible for

defining and documenting requirements for a variety of equity compensation plan-related products and services used by our corporate clients and their plan participants, with a specific focus on the etrade.com user experience.  The Product Manager is responsible for all aspects of product planning and must work closely with diverse teams including client management, customer support, and training to develop and deliver innovative solutions.

Position Summary

 

E*TRADE FINANCIAL Corporate Services is seeking a professional experienced in product development and architecture to fill the role of Product Architect in its Product Management team. This individual will be responsible for defining and documenting requirements for a variety of international equity plan-related products and services used by our corporate clients and their plan participants. Additionally, this person will work with cross-functional teams to define and manage initiatives that involve enhancements to the Corporate Services product offering, including projects and programs aimed at enhancing revenue, gaining competitive advantages, reducing costs, insuring regulatory/statutory compliance, and/or improving service. This job entails end-to-end responsibility for the product development lifecycle for individual projects, from translating business requirements delivered by the Product Planning team into detailed functional requirements, to independently working with the technology and quality assurance teams on design, implementation and testing, to handoff to the Product Support team for internal and customer rollout.  This position will also support the Product Support team as a subject matter expert for product maintenance and production issue escalation.

 

This role requires a combination of strategic and tactical problem solving, coupled with strong execution capability. The successful candidate will be an experienced self-starter and have excellent project management skills, with a proven track record of achieving success in a fast-paced, results-driven organization.

 

Position Objectives

 

·         Demonstrate strong product development functional knowledge, including an in-depth understanding of the processes, tools and techniques essential to delivering projects on-time and within budget

·         Drive product development strategy for customer web experience related product features

·         Acquire intimate domain knowledge of existing Corporate Services products and services, becoming a subject matter expert with respect to current and anticipated functionality, workflows and integration points with other E*TRADE systems

·         Identify and analyze functional deficiencies in our current product offering, and recommend corrective actions

·         Translate analysis into functional requirements documentation for existing and future Corporate Services applications

·         Partner closely with a wide range of stakeholders – including Product Planning, Operations, Customer Service, Marketing, Technology and Quality Assurance - to ensure that product design and implementation accurately reflect the full range of market, business and functional requirements

·         Use judgment and analysis to help prioritize areas for product enhancement

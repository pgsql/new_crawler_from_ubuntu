Job Title EMR Resources Specialist
Department Name SMH Administration
Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

To provide our Physicians and Clinicians the support and communication necessary to the development and success of a fully intergrated, functional information system:

  • By assuring the performance, availability, reliability, of 24/7 critical clinical computing systems necessary to support the delivery of quality patient care. 
  • By maintaining highest level of expertise in knowledge of clinical systems in the Health Care environment.
  • By providing leadership to other team members and serving as a resource to management by leading projects in area of expertise.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Current State of Louisiana RN or LPN license to practice professional nursing.
  • Requires at least three years of experience in direct patient care in an acute care setting; preferably with at least two years of experience with direct contact with clinician computer use. 
  • Understanding of effective Customer Relationship Management Skills necessary for efficient problem resolution to maximize customer satisfaction.

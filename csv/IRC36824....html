Detailed Description

E*TRADE seeks to recruit a Manager within the Treasury group. The Treasury group is responsible for all Treasury related functions within E*TRADE Financial including Portfolio Management, Capital Planning, Cash Management, and Funding & Liquidity Management. This position will support the Director of Treasury with strategic and project related analysis including cash and capital optimization with the companies various legal entities. This person should be a results-oriented, proactive finance professional with an ability to assess and address strategic imperatives and time-critical situations.

 

Responsibilities include:

  • Develops models, analyses, presentations and reports related to Treasury and overall balance sheet strategy and structure

  • Monitors compliance with debt covenants

  • Assist in preparation of rating agency presentations and management of relationships

  • Assist in analyzing debt and/or equity opportunities to optimize the Company’s capital structure

  • Provide support on special projects to optimize the capital and cash within the Company’s various legal entities

  • Interfaces with other functional organizations to provide forecast information and to obtain information and/or decisions needed by Treasury
Job Requirements
  • Bachelor’s degree in finance or accounting, MBA in Finance or CFA a plus

  • 2 to 4  year’s experience in Treasury or Corporate Finance

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • Experience with financial modeling, developing and using financial modeling tools
  • Comfortable working under pressure and able to multi-task and manage competing priorities
  • Strong working knowledge of both corporate finance and accounting

Detailed Description

This position is responsible for the support of the implementation specialist and management of several concurrent projects. Projects include the implementation of Stock Plan Administration and Employee Broker Services for Fortune 1000 corporate clients in a timely and high quality fashion. 

This highly visible position involves interacting with a variety of individuals, internally and externally, at all levels of the organization, including the C-Suit.  This person will work with implementation specialist as a liaison for the implementation team to ensure that client’s data is migrated in full compliance with company guidelines. This will require that they lead discussions with the client that are pertinent to data conversions and that the data is converted in a timely manner.

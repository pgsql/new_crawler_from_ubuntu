Detailed Description

POSITION PURPOSE:

 

Provide timely, relevant, and actionable information to Customer Service and the Company regarding the effectiveness, efficiency, and value of CS, Sales, Marketing, and Operations initiatives.

 

 

ORGANIZATION FIT:

 

Reports to the Manager of Business Reporting; however, this position also supports and works with, members of the Customer Service, Sales, Marketing and Operations management groups. This includes Director Level down to the floor managers as well as other Project Managers and Business Reporting Analysts.

 

 

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

 

<   Assists the Customer Service Director in developing metrics that demonstrate the Contact Center's strategic value to the company.

<   Identifies on an ongoing basis innovative methods for measuring company effectiveness and efficiency.

<   Understands what data the various call center and company data sources contain and combines as required.

<   Assists Customer Service Managers with associate activity details and drilldowns, including agent metrics, interaction reports, customer workflow requests, trade monitoring reporting, and compliance initiatives.

<   Works with Managers / Analyst Teams on developing report definitions, data accuracy, and performing validation as required and necessary.

<   Actively seeks out ways to automate report generation whenever possible by making information available via Business Objects, internal wiki’s / webpages, and SQL scripts.

<   Creates a backup of important company reports (i.e., score cards, executive summaries, etc.).

<   Ensures that the statistical reports produced are accurate, relevant, complete, and actionable.

<   Produces reports that are aesthetically pleasing and easily understood.

<   Creates productivity and efficiency reports that measure individual employee performance.

<   Conducts special analysis projects and creates other reports as directed.

<   Gathers the necessary data to calculate cost per transaction for all transaction types (i.e., e-mail, calls, etc.).

 

 

JOB SETTING:

 

The Business Reporting Analyst is located in Alpharetta, GA.  The environment is ever changing and fast-paced.

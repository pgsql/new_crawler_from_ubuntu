Detailed Description

This position will report directly to the Director of Campaign Execution and Reporting. He/she will be responsible for guiding the efforts of a marketing fulfillment system implementation, and managing the launch of promotions that leverage the marketing fulfillment system.

    • Work closely with marketing partners to define specifications for new promotions;  work with product and technology to develop and deploy new promotions

    • Work collaboratively with product management and technology to develop requirements for new promotion functionality;  participate in User Testing to ensure new promotion functionality meets requirements

    • Partner with customer service and operations to research questions around promotion requirements, qualifications, and fulfillments;  work with the product & technology teams to resolve problems.

    • Serve as subject matter expert within marketing group for all promotion system questions.

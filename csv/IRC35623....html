Detailed Description

Responsible for all domestic and international tax compliance and planning activities for Etrade Clearing LLC operations.  This position will supervise and manage the activities of tax group members, as well as providing tax guidance to other operational departments. This position will be heavily involved in strategic and tactical projects with a focus on client experience and their interactions with the data.

Manager will be a hands on partner with Cost Basis team and will be responsible for tax data integrity for transactions processed within the portfolio accounting systems and the production of the 1099 & 1042S Forms & Tax Packages. The Tax Manager will also help assist an operational group through the year-end tax reporting process and will understand controls and participate in reviews to provide accurate tax reporting for Etrade Bank and Etrade Clearing LLC clients.

Manage and develop staff through effective and timely supervision, training, performance management and appraisal. Create employee development plans and provide opportunities for personnel to enhance their tax, accounting, and other technical skills.

Manage vendor and outsource support functions related to state and federal tax items; ensure all current and deferred tax accounts are properly reconciled and accurately computed. 

Oversee tax compliance; ensure that all federal tax items are properly reported and accounted for; manage IRS audits and tax controversy related to international or domestic items,

Develop, assess, and implement global tax planning projects to maximize resources that supports business objectives, design process improvements, and oversee and maintain tax compliance structures.

Design and lead the implementation of optimal tax procedures, controls and self audit functions. Assist in the analysis and planning of cost basis processing and 1099B reporting.

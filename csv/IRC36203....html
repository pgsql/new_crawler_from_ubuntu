Detailed Description

SUMMARY

E*Trade Financial is looking for a Senior level Engineer to help create and maintain UNIX based servers that power the E*Trade Financial business lines.  The ideal candidate is highly motivated and goal oriented, has the ability to handle interruptions while fluidly switching between several projects, and has a work smart not harder approach while maintaining a meticulous and organized approach to work.

 

In addition, the candidate will participate in research, design and implementation of various tools and technologies that enhance and/or automate different areas within our UNIX Infrastructure environment.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

Work within the group maintaining and developing Infrastructure and not limited to the following:

·         Develop and extend tools to automate, maintain and monitor deployments.

·         Assess, document, present and mentor project assignments.

·         Evaluate and recommend commercial and open source Software.

·         Escalation point to answer System related questions.

·         Automating complex processes.

·         Train and Mentor Team members, System Administrators and Operators.
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·         5+ years experience with UNIX/Linux, strong understanding of Linux OS.

·         4+ years running large production systems (Several hundred).

·         4+ years of strong programming/scripting skills in at least two of the following: Perl, Python, Korn, C.

·         Expert level knowledge in either Virtualization or Clustering solutions

 

OTHER DESIRED SKILLS

·         Virtualization Experience: (e.g., KVM, Xen,  Openvz, lxc, ec2, openstack).

·         Configuration management experience:  (e.g., cfengine, puppet, chef, Git, Subversion.)

·         Disk/Filesystem Management Experience: (e.g., LVM, ASM, GFS, CLVM, Gluster).

·         Cluster Configuration/Management Experience (e.g., Symantec VCS, Linux-HA).

·         Core Services experience: (e.g., Apache, Tomcat, Jetty, Sendmail, Postfix, xntpd, bind).

·         Knowledgeable in Linux packaging tools(e.g.,  rpm, yum, dpkg, apt)

·         Understanding of fundamental Internet Protocols (e.g., DNS, HTTP, SSH, SMTP, FTP).

·         Web/Internet Security experience is a plus.

·         Storage Management and troubleshooting.

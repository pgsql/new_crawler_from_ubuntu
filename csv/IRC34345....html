Detailed Description

SUMMARY

Staff, Operations Engineer will be responsible for monitoring, troubleshooting, upgrading, level 2/3 problem resolution of large WAN/LAN environment including Cisco routers, Cisco switches, firewalls, VPN and VOIP.  No administration of systems is involved .  This role is for networking only and requires knowledge of Cisco networking.

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Coordinate with network and system teams to implement network changes including building configurations for connectivity through WAN, LAN and firewalls.

·         Maintain network devices including upgrades, physical replacement and configuration standards.

·         Capability to create concise documentation of issues, compile statistics and build reports on network performance is required.

·         Shift:  Monday through Friday in the Network operations center in Alpharetta Georgia - flexibility required.  This position operates in a team environment; self-motivation, a sense of urgency and a good working ethic/attitude are a must to be successful in this environment.
Job Requirements

REQUIREMENTS

·         Candidate must demonstrate initiative, self-motivation and possess a strong sense of urgency to be considered.

·         An understanding of Layer 2 and Layer 3 troubleshooting and integration.

·         Exposure to firewall troubleshooting is preferred but not required.

·         Exposure to maintaining, upgrading and configure L2/L3 Cisco switches, routers and firewalls.

·         WAN experience with point to point, Ethernet and MPLS preferred.

·         VOIP, VPLS and encryption experience as well as experience with network management/monitoring applications and sniffer applications in an operational environment preferred.

·         Bachelor Degree or equivalent and 2+ years of hands on experience working in an enterprise operational LAN/WAN environment.

·         CCNA preferred

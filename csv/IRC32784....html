SUMMARY

The Senior Staff, Systems Engineer will work with the Windows Servers group within the Enterprise Engineering team.  It is an Enterprise grade environment with over a 1000 servers. 

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

The Engineer will be responsible for:

·         Build, deploy, and maintain a variety of automated systems

·         Interact with many tech and non-tech resources internally as well as vendors and external resources in the Microsoft Windows realm. 

·         Create and document systems/application architecture,

·         Manage complex technical projects and interfacing with business and technology groups. 

·         Transition between a business and tech role as well as a lead vs. resource role.

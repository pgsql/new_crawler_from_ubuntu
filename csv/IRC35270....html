Detailed Description

Position Summary

Work with our existing customers to satisfy their more complex transactional advice needs.  Advisors will offer objective and unbiased investment advice and guidance.  Client needs will be matched with non-proprietary financial products, services and solutions to optimize portfolio allocation.

Advisors must always put customer needs in front or their own.

 

Responsibilities

  • Work with existing clients to build or supplement their portfolios by assessing their investment objectives, risk tolerance, time horizon and unique circumstances
  • Minimal prospecting, with abundance of existing client accounts to cultivate
  • Satisfy customer needs with a wide range or investment products and services
  • Work on creating relationship that will win a larger portion of the customers assets
  • Core products competencies must include

    • Conservative Investments (Money Markets, BCDs and Treasuries)

    • Fixed Income (BCDs, Corporate, Municipal, Treasury, Agency and misc. other bonds)

    • Mutual Funds (Approved list)

    • Structured products

    • Fee based products (Managed Investment Portfolios, UMA/SMA, etc.)

  • Future products may include more robust 529 Plans and Insurance products

  • Solid understanding of financial planning, portfolio management and asset allocation process.

  • Must maintain strong relationships with E*Trade Branches and Financial Consultants to assist with their client segments.
  • Future products may include more robust 529 Plans and Insurance products
Job Requirements

Requirements:

  • 3-5 years of experience as a full service broker, with an emphasis on portfolio allocation and client assets
  • Series 7 & 63 Required. Series 65/66 preferred
  • Highly developed customer service skills and excellent phone skills

  • Self-motivated and have the ability to prioritize responsibilities in a fast paced environment

  • Entrepreneurial mindset and approach desired

  • Bachelors degree preferred

Company Overview

Detailed Description

SUMMARY

The Senior Staff, Support Analyst will provide functional support of applications within the e-Business suite. Core activities will include gathering and defining requirements, configuration of standard applications, gap analysis, functional design of application extensions, and end user support for multiple modules in multiple geographies. Experience with Oracle Financials R12 product suite (GL, AP, AR, PO, FA, CE, Contracts, iProcurement, iSupplier and Procurement contracts Modules) is critical to this role.

 

RESPONSIBILITIES

·         Act as the first line analyst in resolving reported Oracle Financial problems. This includes primary analysis and initial interface with the business to diagnose problems and identify potential system or business process solutions.

·         Successfully interact with the technical team on all issues related to the on-going needs of the Business Systems applications.

·         Work on a wide variety of production support issues and set ups that may include applications other than Oracle Projects & Processes.

·         Direct or participate in meetings with the application users and business sponsors to gather and refine business requirements, functional designs and use-cases for projects/upgrades that impact or support the Finance department.

·         Run Oracle systems projects including roll out of modules and new development. 

·         Interacts with technical development team when required to develop custom programs or reports.

·         Participate in new system implementations and financial system upgrades.

·         Coordinate and verifies all system testing for system upgrades, enhancements, and implementations.

·         Identify potential issues in terms of business practices/processes and end user adoption and suggests alternative solutions.

·         Perform detailed data analysis and presents findings in a cohesive, high impact format to drive and inform business and technical decisions, and to estimate the impact of data changes or user-requested enhancements.

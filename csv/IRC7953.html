Job Title Respiratory Therapy Tech-CRTT
Department Name St. Martin Hospital - Respiratory Therapy
Job Requirements
POSITION SUMMARY:

Under the supervision of the Manager of Respiratory Care Services, a Respiratory Therapist performs a variety of duties to carry out the medical prescription of a physician in the diagnosis and treatment of cardiopulmonary disease.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Current license by the Louisiana State Board of Medical Examiners as a Certified-eligible, Certified, or Registered Therapist required.
  • CPR, Falls Prevention, OSHA, & Restraints are to be completed with in 30 days from hire and are renewable annually.

Job Title Infection Preventionist
Department Name Outcomes Management
Job Requirements

POSITION SUMMARY:

Responsible for infection prevention functions of surveillance for specific infectious diseases; reporting of cases involving the possible spread of infection; outbreak investigation; analysis and interpretation of data concerning epidemiologic investigations; institution of prevention measures; and program evaluation.

EDUCATION / TRAINING / EXPERIENCE:

  • Current license or registration as a medical technologist or clinical laboratory scientist or current licensure as an RN with the State of Louisiana required.
  • Bachelor of Science degree in a healthcare related field.
  • Minimum of five (5) years of hospital based work experience required.
  • One (1) year experience with infection control preferred.
  • Knowledge of regulatory agencies required.
  • Extensive computer knowledge is required.
  • Certification in Infection Prevention preferred at hire.
  • Obtain certification within 2 years of hire if not present upon hire.
  • Demonstrated human relations, effective communication, and public speaking skills required.
  • Strong interpretive, mathematic, and analytical skills required.
  • Ability to organize work and meet deadlines required.
